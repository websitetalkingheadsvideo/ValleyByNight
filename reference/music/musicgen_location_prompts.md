## Hawthorn Estate (Elysium)

**Design Questions:**

- **Energy level**: Low (subtle, contemplative) / Medium (present but unobtrusive) / High (dramatic, noticeable presence)
- **Instrumentation bias**: Strings (classical elegance) / Piano (intimate, refined) / Jazz elements (sophisticated lounge) / Dark synth (modern gothic) / Choir pad (ethereal, otherworldly) / Mixed (layered textures)
- **Warm vs cool palette**: Warm (golden candlelight, rich mahogany tones) / Cool (teal noir, moonlight through windows) / Neutral (balanced, neither warm nor cool)
- **Bed vs Feature**: Bed (background under dialogue, loopable underscore) / Feature (noticeable theme, can have subtle dynamics)
- **Tempo guidance**: Very slow (meditative, time-stretching) / Slow (stately, processional) / Mid (moderate, conversational pace)
- **Diegetic texture**: None / Murmured court conversations (distant, indistinct) / Glass clink (crystal, refined) / Distant fountain (water feature ambience) / Fireplace crackle (warmth, comfort)

**Selected Choices:**
- Energy: Medium
- Instrumentation: Old world instruments (strings, piano, classical)
- Palette: Warm (golden candlelight, amber tones)
- Type: Bed (background under dialogue)
- Tempo: Medium-slow
- Diegetic: Footsteps (some steps), murmured conversations, distant fountain

**Prompt v1:**
```
RPG location ambience, background under dialogue, loopable underscore. Medium energy, medium-slow tempo. Old world classical instrumentation: warm string ensemble (violins, cellos), subtle piano, gentle harp accents. Analog warmth, live acoustic recording with slight imperfections, tape saturation. Golden candlelight palette, rich mahogany tones, amber glow. Sophisticated tension, beauty serving as camouflage. Non-distracting, supports conversation. Subtle evolving textures, multiple sections for dynamic arrangement. Desert gothic elegance, 1994 neo-noir atmosphere. Diegetic ambience: occasional footsteps on marble, distant murmured conversations, subtle fountain water texture. No vocals, no percussion. Room tone, natural reverb of grand hall.
```

**Prompt v1-alt:**
```
Cinematic intro bed, RPG location underscore, loopable background music. Medium energy, stately medium-slow tempo. Classical chamber music: cello and viola duet, distant piano, sparse string quartet. Live performance texture, analog warmth, slight bow imperfections, natural room resonance. Warm golden palette, candlelit ambience, rich wood tones. Gothic elegance, refined danger. Background under dialogue, non-intrusive. Evolving arrangement with subtle textural shifts. Desert estate atmosphere, 1994 setting. Diegetic elements: footsteps on polished floor, hushed conversations in background, distant courtyard fountain. No drums, no vocals. Tape hiss, vintage recording character.
```

**Iteration Knobs:**
- **Slower**: Reduce tempo to "slow, stately processional"
- **More strings**: Emphasize "full string section, layered violins"
- **Less piano**: Remove piano or change to "distant, barely audible"
- **More desert texture**: Add "subtle wind texture, distant cicadas, dry air resonance"
- **Warmer palette**: Emphasize "amber, gold, firelight tones, mahogany warmth"
- **More reverb**: Add "cathedral-like reverb, vaulted ceiling echo"
- **Subtle choir pad**: Add "distant ethereal pad, wordless voices, barely perceptible"

# Tremere — Phoenix Chantry & Occult Landscape (1995)

## Revised Chantry Hierarchy

- **Helena Crowly (Primogen):** The "Grandmother" and Arcane Authority. She protects the Prince and the City's borders.
- **James Whitmore (Regent):** The "CEO." He manages the neonates and the money, but he avoids Helena's basement lab.
- **Dr. Margaret Ashford:** The "Internal Auditor." She is likely Helena's rival. While Helena looks out at the city, Margaret looks in at the clan's purity.
- **Andrei Radulescu:** The "Golden Boy." He has the protection of both the Regent (for his work) and the Primogen (as her favorite student). This makes him the most envied apprentice in the Chantry.

With a strong presence of both Setites and Giovanni in the city, Phoenix is a hotbed of the occult.

In 1995, Phoenix is a "neutral" gold mine: the Camarilla holds the towers, but the Setites and Giovanni own the foundations. This creates a **Cold War of the Occult**, where Helena Crowly and James Whitmore are constantly playing defense against two clans that specialize in corruption and the afterlife.

## 1. The Setite Contention: The "Tempe" Influence

The Followers of Set thrive in 1995 Phoenix. With the city's rapid sprawl and the burgeoning university scene in Tempe, they have a constant supply of "seekers" and "sinners."

- **Conflict with Helena:** Helena's 19th-century Egyptian relics are a religious insult to the local Setites. They likely view her "Scholar of the Arts" title as a thief's mask.
- **1995 Vibe:** The Setites likely control the "Underground Rave" scene and the early "New Age" bookstores in Sedona and Scottsdale. While the Camarilla focuses on politics, the Setites are rotting the mortal moral fabric from the bottom up.
- **Occult Threat:** They aren't just drug dealers; they are masters of Serpentis and Setite Sorcery. They are likely the ones testing Helena's wards with "whispering sands" or subtle curses that bypass Hermetic circles.

## 2. The Giovanni Interest: The "City of the Dead"

Phoenix is built literally on top of the ancient Hohokam ruins (the "Canal Makers"). To the Giovanni, the city is a necromantic buffet.

- **Business Front:** In 1995, the Giovanni likely have their hands in the booming Phoenix real estate and funeral home industries. They are the "silent partners" in the city's expansion, ensuring they have access to every fresh grave.
- **Conflict with the Tremere:** The Tremere and Giovanni are the two great "Usurper" clans. There is zero trust. While the Tremere use *Thaumaturgy* to control the living blood, the Giovanni use *Necromancy* to listen to the ghosts of the Hohokam.
- **"Breach" Theory:** If someone bypassed Helena's wards without a sound, it was likely someone who didn't walk through the front door—a Wraith commanded by a Giovanni Necromancer.

## 3. The "Neutral" Ground: Bayside Bob's & The Giovanni

While Bayside Bob (Toreador) runs an Anarch clubhouse, in a city this hot, even the Independents need a drink.

- The Giovanni might use Bob's neutrality to broker "travel papers" for Kindred moving through the desert.
- The Setites might use the tiki-bar aesthetic to mask their own "Temple of Joy."

## 4. Setting Supplement: Helena's Forensic Focus

In this environment, Helena's role as a Forensic Thaumaturge is critical. In a normal city she'd look for fingerprints; in Phoenix, she's looking for:

| Evidence | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| **Ectoplasmic Residue** | Evidence of Giovanni ghost-work. |
| **Heart-corruption** | Evidence of Setite *Serpentis* influence on the Prince's ghouls. |
| **Ancient Resonances** | The Hohokam canals act as "Ley Lines." If the Setites or Giovanni tap into these, they could potentially circumvent the Tremere Chantry's power grid. |

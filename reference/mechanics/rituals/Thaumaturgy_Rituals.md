# Thaumaturgy Rituals

*A comprehensive guide to Thaumaturgical rituals organized by rank*

---

## Rank 1 Rituals

### Bind the Accusing Tongue [1]

**Ingredients:** A lock of hair of the target, a black silk cord, and a picture of the person in question  
**Process:** Wind the cord around the hair and image while incanting.  
**Effect:** The target cannot speak ill about the caster.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Blood Contract [2]

**Ingredients:** A contract written in the blood of the caster, another party  
**Process:** The other party applies its name in blood on the contract.  
**Effect:** The caster creates a binding contract between the two parties that will unleash terrible consequences on the one who breaks the letter of the contract.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Blood into Water [3]

**Ingredients:** A cup of purified water  
**Process:** Pour the water through your outstretched hand while incanting.  
**Effect:** Transform all spilt blood in a radius around you into water.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Blood Mastery [4]

**Ingredients:** A vial of vitae of both the caster and the target  
**Process:** Burn the vitae over an open flame while incanting.  
**Effect:** Gain one granted success against the target in a specific endeavor.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Blood of Peace [5]

**Ingredients:** Two Points of Caster's Blood. 10 minutes.  
**Process:** Spike a Drink or apply to the base skin.  
**Effect:** Blood becomes a Potent drug that induces deep sleep  
**Developer:** Clan Banu Haqim

---

### Blood Rush [6]

**Ingredients:** The fang of a predatory animal  
**Process:** Incanting.  
**Effect:** Feel the pleasure of drinking blood without actually doing so.  
**Developer:** Sabbat

---

### Brand [7]

**Ingredients:** Molten silver, a tattoo or something similar  
**Process:** While incanting, molten silver is poured over the tattoo.  
**Effect:** The tattoo becomes permanent.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Brand of the Paramour [8]

**Ingredients:** Mortal twins, a ghoul  
**Process:** Both caster and ghoul drink two blood points from the twins.  
**Effect:** The caster feels sympathetic pain when the ghoul is harmed.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### The Carrion Fly Delayed [9]

**Ingredients:** A living victim, a censor, alchemical salts  
**Process:** Vivisect the victim, then put the extracted parts above the censor filled with the salts.  
**Effect:** Preserve severed body parts from rot.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Chime of Unseen Spirits [10]

**Ingredients:** The wedding rings from three dead women  
**Process:** The rings are melted into a chime whilst the caster incants.  
**Effect:** The chimes will ring when a spiritual entity is nearby.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Cloak of Blood [11]

**Ingredients:** An innocent  
**Process:** Fasting for seven nights, then drink blood freely offered from the innocent.  
**Effect:** Temporarily mask the stains of Diablerie in your aura.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Communicate with Sire [12]

**Effect:** Communicate telepathically with your direct sire.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

### Counting Coup [13]

### Now Its Sight Is Ours [30]

**Ingredients:** A three-inch golden pin, wire, vitae from the caster, a golden bowl, a censor, a small golden claw, and a living victim  
**Process:** The victim has its eye plucked out, which is then slowly pierced with the pin under incantations. The pin is attached to the wire, which is attached to the claw. The eye is drawn through the smoke of the censor and then bathed in the vitae within the bowl.  
**Effect:** The eye can be used as a spying device when a vampire drinks something from the vitae the eye was bathed in.  
**Developer:** Paul Cordwood

---

### Preserve Blood [31]

**Ingredients:** An earthenware jug

---

### Preserve Corpse [32]

**Ingredients:** A special liquid composed of decaying organic material and rare herbs

**Ingredients:** A specially constructed gravesite, a dead body, incense

### Your Fate Shall be Known [98]

**Ingredients:** A willing participant, a golden needle, an agate, alchemical vapors  
**Process:** The caster pierces the eyeball of the participant and extracts a small amount of vitreous gel from it. The gel is rubbed on the agate while the caster incants. Over the course of a month, the agate is steamed under alchemical vapors until an eyelid-like shell has developed around it.  
**Effect:** The eye shows the state of its donor. When the donor sleeps or is incapacitated, it closes. When the donor dies, it crumbles to dust.

# Thaumaturgy Rituals - Ranks 3-5

*A comprehensive guide to Thaumaturgical rituals for Ranks 3, 4, and 5*

---

## Rank 3 Rituals

### Amulet of Mnemosyne [99]

**Ingredients:** An amulet made out of brass, blood, a symbol of truth  
**Process:** The caster bathes the amulet in blood, then places it upon the symbol, kisses it and whispers a secret into it.  
**Effect:** The caster can store a memory within the amulet.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Animated Assistants [100]

**Ingredients:** A knife  
**Process:** The caster cuts their arms while incanting.  
**Effect:** Items around the caster accomplish minor tasks for them.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Awakening the Verdant Thorn [101]

**Ingredients:** A stake, a fire  
**Process:** The caster hardens the stake above the fire and chants the incantation. Afterwards, the caster pierces their own heart.  
**Effect:** The stake will sprout into a mass of thorny vines when coming in contact with a vampire, draining them of their vitae.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere (adopted from Sielanic Thaumaturgy)

---

### Beacon of the Self [102]

**Ingredients:** A portion of the victim  
**Process:** The caster incants, then the portion transforms into yellow smoke. The caster inhales the smoke.  
**Effect:** The caster receives a dream-image of the current location of the victim.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Bind the Familiar [103]

**Ingredients:** Blood, a small animal  
**Process:** Beginning at dusk, the caster draws a circle out of blood, drawing their True Name at its circumference. At midnight, the caster drains the animal to house the spirit dry and places it within the circle, feeding it a small portion of their own vitae. After incanting for several hours, a spirit will be drawn to the body and trapped in the circle.  
**Effect:** The caster binds a spirit to act as a Familiar.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Bladed Hands [104]

**Process:** Incantation.  
**Effect:** Make your hand's edges sharp as knives.  
**Developer:** Victor

### Blood Allergy [105]

**Process:** The caster spills their blood into the petals.

### Bottled Voice [106]

**Ingredients:** A crystal bottle, wax

**Effect:** The target loses their voice.

### Brotherhood of the Cup [107]

**Ingredients:** A chalice  
**Process:** The caster, as well as up to four other vampires, pour their blood in the chalice. The caster speaks an incantation, and afterwards, all share a drink from the cup, similar to the Vaulderie.  
**Effect:** The participants are filled with intense loyalty to Clan Tremere and what they perceive to be its bests interests.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Crucible of Sympathetic Agony [112]

**Ingredients:** Two persons, an iron rack, sewing needles, various torture instruments  
**Process:** The caster sews the soles of the feet of the two victims together and places them on the rack. Afterwards, one or both of the victims are forced to climax (by whatever means). Then, one of the two is tortured.  
**Effect:** The second victim feels the same pain as the other and if the tortured victim should die, the second victim will temporally feel how it is to die.  
**Developer:** Mendacamina

---

### Curse of the Outcast [113]

**Ingredients:** A victim, pieces of paper  
**Process:** Over a period of ten days, the caster collects pieces of paper that have been in contact with the victim. He must then write on the paper and burn it. On the eleventh day, the caster fasts and performs the ritual.  
**Effect:** The victim's difficulty for all social rolls is raised for a month (more quickly when the victim leaves the area near the caster).  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere (adopted from Feng Shui)

**Ingredients:** A weapon, skin of a scavenger animal

**Ingredients:** Vampires of either the Tzimisce, Gangrel, or Nosferatu Clan (others have been tested with no success), a sac for the gestation, alchemical liquids

Over three months, various incantations have to be made over the gestating creature. When the creature is large enough to break free from the sac, the caster blows purifying smoke at the newborn.

# Assamite Ritual Quick-Reference

## Rituals by Level

### Level 1 Rituals

#### Touch the Earth
**Cost/Duration:** 1 Blood; One Scene

Sense a target's general location or state by touching soil.

---

#### Pebble From the Mountain
**Cost/Duration:** Constant link

Link a stone from Alamut to a brother/sister to track them.

---

#### Truth in Ink
**Cost/Duration:** Permanent ink

Identify forgeries or magically bind a contract in truth.

---

#### Warding Baptism
**Cost/Duration:** 1 Blood; Permanent

Protects a location/person from specific spiritual intrusions.

---

#### Strength of Haqim
**Cost/Duration:** 1 Blood; One Scene

Temporarily boosts physical resilience or capacity.

---

### Level 2 Rituals

#### Astral Portent
**Cost/Duration:** Mental Challenge

Reading stars or signs for divination and omens.

---

#### Blood Calls to Blood
**Cost/Duration:** 1 Blood; 1 Night

Track individuals through related lineage or specific vitae.

---

#### Gift of Mithra's Bull
**Cost/Duration:** 2 Blood; One Night

Enhances physical prowess/endurance of the recipient.

---

### Level 3 Rituals

#### Approach the Veil
**Cost/Duration:** 1 Blood; One Scene

Makes the subject significantly harder to detect or target.

---

#### Rite of Marduk
**Cost/Duration:** 2 Blood; Instant

A combat-focused ritual used to break curses or grant victory.

---

### Level 4 Rituals

#### Seeing with Sky's Eyes
**Cost/Duration:** 2 Blood; One Hour

Grants aerial or long-distance vision over a target area.

---

#### Ahriman's Lance
**Cost/Duration:** 2 Blood; One Attack

Enhances accuracy and lethality of ranged hunter attacks.

---

### Level 5 Rituals

#### Blood Contract
**Cost/Duration:** 3 Blood; Permanent

Mystical binding pact between parties; breakage causes agony.

---

#### Hunter's Wind
**Cost/Duration:** 3 Blood; One Scene

Manipulates travel and distance for the sorcerer or a group.

---

## Key Rules for Play

### Kalif Dependency
Invoking any ritual requires 1 point of kalif-influenced vitae or inhaling smoke to enter the necessary trance.

### Challenge Mechanics
Success usually requires a Mental Challenge against the target or a static number of traits.

### Casting Time
Standard rituals take 5 minutes per level to cast unless otherwise noted.

### Foreign Magic
Assamites pay 50% more XP and extra blood to learn rituals from other traditions (like Tremere Thaumaturgy).

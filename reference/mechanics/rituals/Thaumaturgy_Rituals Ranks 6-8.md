# Thaumaturgy Rituals - Ranks 6-8

*A comprehensive guide to Thaumaturgical rituals for Ranks 6, 7, and 8*

---

## Rank 6 Rituals

### Bone of Contention [249]

**Ingredients:** A 200 year old finger bone  
**Process:** The caster incantates and washes the bone in blood.  
**Effect:** The Bone, while behaving like a Bone of Lies, is actually controlled by the caster.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### The Eternal Mask [250]

**Ingredients:** A victim, proficient powers to read Auras  
**Process:** The caster studies the Aura of the victim for several hours before ritually committing Diablerie on them.  
**Effect:** The caster absorbs the target's aura to use it for themselves.  
**Developer:** Kemintiri

---

### Evade Curse [251]

**Ingredients:** (Not specified)  
**Process:** 5 Minute Ritual While feeding  
**Effect:** Duration number of turns equal to Successes on a Stamina + Occult roll. Allows a Banu Haqim under their Curse of Tyre to tolerate the blood of other Kindred  
**Developer:** Clan Banu Haqim

---

### The Gift [252]

**Ingredients:** (Not specified)  
**Process:** Laying of Hands. 5 minutes per dot given of the Discipline.  
**Effect:** The Caster can grant part of her own Disciplines to another.  
**Developer:** Sabbat

---

### Greater Scrying [253]

**Ingredients:** A pool of water, a statue of Apollo, astrological symbols, something associated with the thing you want to observe  
**Process:** The caster holds the object and incantates before the pool of water.  
**Effect:** The caster can use mental disciplines and rituals through the scrying.  
**Developer:** Inconnu

---

### Iron Mind [254]

**Ingredients:** Earing, hat pin, or other object containing iron  
**Process:** Ritual cast on the object and then worn on the head.  
**Effect:** Makes the target invulnerable to Auspex for one night. The individual using Auspex will know something is wrong but not what.  
**Developer:** Sabbat

---

### Lost Life

**Ingredients:** An Object to be Cursed.  
**Process:** The caster spends two hours a night for two weeks preparing an object. There can be no distractions and at the end, the caster must spend 3 Willpower points and five points of blood. They caster must know Chimerstry for the Curse to work.  
**Effect:** Receiver immediately expels blood through all possible means. He loses half of his blood pool and the process it is very painful. Stamina roll (difficulty 7 to 8) to avoid losing half his health levels as well; Self-Control roll to avoid Frenzy. The effects last until the next sunset and until that time the character may not increase his blood pool.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Mass Grave [255]

**Ingredients:** A grave with dirt that contains at least one sentient creature's remains  
**Process:** Buried  
**Effect:** Passage to the Shadowlands.  
**Developer:** (Not specified)

---

### Quenching the Lambent Flame [256]

**Ingredients:** A vampire above the 13th Generation, blood of both the caster and the target  
**Process:** The target has to lie motionless in a circle drawn with the blood of the caster and the blood of the target.  
**Effect:** The Generation of the target is raised to the 13th.  
**Developer:** Mictlantecuhtli

---

### Raise the Dead [257]

**Ingredients:** A corpse, black candle wax, a circle of salt  
**Process:** The caster pours the candle wax into the orifices of the corpse within the circle and inscribed the forehead with a symbol meaning "debtor" while in complete darkness.  
**Effect:** The corpse rises as an undead servant.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Refined Digestion [258]

**Ingredients:** Blood from the target and the caster  
**Process:** The caster incantates while mixing the blood.  
**Effect:** The target suffers the Thirst of Ages, only gaining sustenance from Cainite vitae.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Ritual of Demesne [259]

**Ingredients:** Sigils symbolizing the domain, blood  
**Process:** The caster draws the sigils in blood across all major entry points (airports, train stations, highways) of a given perimeter.  
**Effect:** Whenever a vampire crosses the perimeters of the ritual, the caster is alerted about their location.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Sands of Time [260]

**Ingredients:** A marble bowl full of white sand and glass shards  
**Process:** The caster incantates over the bowl.  
**Effect:** The sands will slow anything down on which they are sprinkled.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Spider's Web [261]

**Ingredients:** Black Widow Spider  
**Process:** Spider is crushed and consumed.  
**Effect:** Creates a Web that resembles an actual spider's web in all respects, except the web is big enough to fill a large room, and sticky and strong enough to capture and hold victims.  
**Developer:** Sabbat

---

### Utter Destruction of Bonds [262]

**Ingredients:** The caster's tongue, a lockable object  
**Process:** The caster incantates, then tears out their own tongue and smears it across the object.  
**Effect:** The object will never again lock properly.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

## Rank 7 Rituals

### Bone of Eternal Thirst [263]

**Ingredients:** A bone or ivory, three fledglings (one of them the caster's childe)  
**Process:** The caster carves a weapon from the bone and bathes it in the blood of the fledglings over the course of three nights.  
**Effect:** The created weapon deals Aggravated damage and drinks one point of blood from any wounded creature.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Chill of the Windsaber [264]

**Ingredients:** Piece of thin, clear glass  
**Process:** Glass is snapped on two  
**Effect:** Sheet of Telekinetic force decapitates anyone whom the caster point his finger at.  
**Developer:** Sabbat

---

### Divorcing the Soul [265]

**Ingredients:** Dead pomegranate seeds  
**Process:** The caster walks in a circle around the target and drops the seeds, incantating every time a seed is dropped.  
**Effect:** The soul of the target is separated from, but still trapped in, their body.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Eyes of the Ever Vigilant [266]

**Ingredients:** The eyes of seven vampires who have lied to any of the Kindred invoking the ritual, an alchemical solution, a building  
**Process:** The caster puts the eyes into the solution, brews them until nothing remains and lets the vapors pervade the building.  
**Effect:** Within the area, no attempt to use magical disguises or illusions works.  
**Developer:** Inner Council of Seven

---

### Healing Blood [267]

**Ingredients:** Blood Point; 10 minutes ritual without interruption  
**Process:** (Not specified)  
**Effect:** Heal Aggravated Wounds with one point per health level and no WP expenditure.  
**Developer:** Clan Banu Haqim

---

### Impede the Gifts of Caine [268]

**Ingredients:** A small item, blood of at least two thaumaturges  
**Process:** The caster drenches the item in the blood, sets it aflame and puts the fire out with their bare hands.  
**Effect:** The usage of Disciplines around the item becomes more difficult.  
**Developer:** Quaesitori

---

### Platonic Split [269]

**Ingredients:** A vampire  
**Process:** Incantating.  
**Effect:** The vampire becomes divided into two parts, one containing the Beast and one containing the Man.  
**Developer:** Cretheus

---

### Ritual of the Severed Hand [270]

**Ingredients:** A vampire under the Vinculum, an alchemical mixture made out of the caster's blood, powdered human bone, the ashes of a vampire destroyed by sunlight, and various crushed herbs  
**Process:** The caster forces the mixture down the motionless target's throat, then watches if an extremity turns black, then severs it.  
**Effect:** All Vinculi on the target are broken.  
**Developer:** Sabbat Inquisition

---

### Shadow of the Wolf [271]

**Ingredients:** Cloak made from the skin of a wolf.  
**Process:** Wearing of the cloak.  
**Effect:** This ritual lets the vampire become a Lupine for one night. See Skin Dancer and the Ritual of Sacred Rebirth  
**Developer:** Sabbat

---

### Whip of the Erinyes [272]

**Ingredients:** A victim  
**Process:** Incantating.  
**Effect:** The target becomes subject to harrowing memories of past transgression and crimes.  
**Developer:** Cretheus

---

## Rank 8 Rituals

### Blade of the Forbidden Flower [273]

**Ingredients:** A drained vampire able in the use of a weapon, a seven-times forged weapon  
**Process:** The caster drains the last drop of blood from the vampire and drives the weapon into the corpse.  
**Effect:** The soul of the target becomes trapped in the weapon, granting its wielder all its Abilities and Disciplines. The created weapon deals Aggravated damage.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### Chain of the Bloodline [274]

**Ingredients:** A vampire who has created progeny  
**Process:** The caster drains the vampire of his blood over the course of three nights, then diablerizes the vampire at the new moon.  
**Effect:** The entire brood of the used vampire has a lower resistance to disobey commands from the caster.  
**Developer:** Clan Tremere

---

### The Name Forgotten [275]

**Ingredients:** A sheet of paper milled from wood grown only by moonlight, blood of the sire  
**Process:** The caster writes their name on the paper, then invests Willpower in it over the next 100 years.  
**Effect:** The caster becomes forgotten by everyone who knew them when they meet Final Death.  
**Developer:** unknown

---

### Soul Disjunction [276]

**Ingredients:** A person with a soul  
**Process:** The caster incantates, beseeching Hermes and Hekate to rip the soul away from an enemy, then, the caster points at the target and says the final magical word.  
**Effect:** The target loses its soul and sinks into torpor.  
**Developer:** Cretheus

# Characters to Create


## Mrs. Chen

**Location:** Four-plex near Dunlap and 32nd Avenue (Malkavian Haven)

**Type:** Ghoul

**Employer:** Missfortune the Malkavian Primogen

**Status:** Needs character creation

**Notes:** Middle-aged woman who owns a four-plex building near Dunlap and 32nd Avenue. She is a ghoul of the Malkavian Primogen and maintains one apartment in the building specifically for short-term Malkavian visitors. She handles all maintenance, cleaning, and preparation between guests. She never asks questions, never comments on the state of the place when guests leave, just cleans up and prepares it for the next arrival. She is aware of the Kindred nature of her employers but maintains strict discretion. The apartment features an upside-down room with furniture glued to the ceiling and fun house mirrors throughout.

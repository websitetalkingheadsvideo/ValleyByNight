# Image Prompt: Rey Gonzalez

## Character Portrait Prompt

Square portrait of Rey Gonzalez, a Brujah from Valley by Night, depicted in a cinematic World of Darkness style. Heavy-set Hispanic man in his mid-40s with a solid, working-class build—muscle layered under comfortable middle-age weight. Short, neat dark hair and dark eyes that hold both warmth and intense focus. Face shows the lines of someone who's spent years organizing and fighting for causes. Wears practical professional attire: muted earth-tone chinos and a polo shirt that suggests casual authority. Strong, capable hands visible. 

Lighting is moody and dramatic, with warm amber light from a single source (perhaps a desk lamp or shop lighting) casting medium-high contrast shadows in cool teal. The background suggests a tailoring shop or industrial workspace—fabric, sewing machines, or work surfaces visible but softly blurred in shallow depth of field. The image feels intimate and realistic, like a film still, with subtle haze and emotional tension. His expression captures that moment between calm professionalism and the Brujah intensity that can surface—a man who knows how to work a room and when to let the fire show.

**Color Palette:**

- Shadows: Cool teal
- Accent: Subtle crimson (suggesting Brujah passion beneath the surface)
- Background: Muted earth tones, industrial grays

**Atmosphere:**

## Completed Characters

### 1. Katherine Zantosa - Revenant ✅
**Template:** Ghoul  
**File:** `Katherine_Zantosa_Revenant.json`  
**Type:** Independent Revenant (Zantosa family)  
**Key Features:**
- 400+ years old, born during Anarch Rebellion
- NOT bonded to any vampire (blood_bond_stage: 0)
- Uses Vicissitude 3 to alter appearance
- Needs vampire blood once per decade
- Runs nation-wide escort agency and business empire
- Blood Pool: 15, uses disciplines like a vampire

### 2. Darnelle Jones - Ghost ✅
**Template:** Wraith  
**File:** `Darnelle_Jones_Ghost.json`  
**Type:** Vengeful ghost  
**Key Features:**
- Law student murdered by vampire in 1994
- Uses Passion Pool (6) instead of Blood Pool
- Can manifest physically for 5 Passion
- Hunts vampires to protect mortals
- Equivalent disciplines: Auspex 2, Chimerstry 2, Dementation 1
- Will ally with high-Humanity vampires

### 3. Alice Beck - Mage ✅
**Template:** Mage  
**File:** `Alice_Beck_Mage.json`  
**Type:** Orphan Mage (serial killer)  
**Key Features:**
- Paradigm: gains power from killing
- Spheres: Mind 3, Prime 4
- Arete 4, Quintessence 10
- Kills criminals once per month (pimps, dealers, muggers)
- Struggling against addiction to killing
- Equivalent: Auspex 3, Fortitude 1, Thaumaturgy 4

### 4. Johann Metzner - Alchemist ✅
**Template:** Mortal  
**File:** `Johann_Metzner_Alchemist.json`  
**Type:** Immortal Alchemist  
**Key Features:**
- 400+ years old (born late 16th century)
- Created potion of immortality from vampire blood
- Current potion lasts ~15 years
- Researching permanent version (needs 100 blood points)
- Alchemical armor (5 rating), aggravated weapons
- Fortitude 3 equivalent through alchemy

### 5. Raging Howl - Werewolf ✅
**Template:** Werewolf (official)  
**File:** `Raging_Howl_Werewolf.json`  
**Type:** Lupus Ahroun werewolf  
**Key Features:**
- Caused death of pack, now hunts vampires as atonement
- Lives as homeless man in city park
- Rage 8, Gnosis 6, Willpower 8
- Extremely dangerous in Crinos form
- Equivalent: Celerity 4, Fortitude 4, Potence 2, Protean 4
- Death wish makes him reckless
- Silver weakness

### 6. Urban Spirit ✅
**Template:** Werewolf (with extensive notes - is actually a spirit)  
**File:** `Urban_Spirit.json`  
**Type:** Corrupted nature spirit  
**Key Features:**
- Was forest spirit, now warped by urbanization
- Uses Essence (15 max, 1/day regeneration)
- Manifestation costs 5 Essence
- Animalism 2, Dementation 4, Fortitude 5, Potence 3
- Spreads madness in neighborhoods
- Can only be killed with spirit-targeting powers

### 7. Aurial - Demon ✅
**Template:** Demon (official)  
**File:** `Aurial_Demon.json`  
**Type:** Devil demon, power broker  
**Key Features:**
- Summoned in 1929 on Black Tuesday
- Creates soul contracts granting Resources 5
- Faith 8, Torment 3
- Possessing mortal host (transformed homeless man)
- Believes vampire souls already damned
- Equivalent: Auspex 2, Chimerstry 3, Daimoinon 4, Obfuscate 3, Vicissitude 3

### 8. Gadreel - Fallen Angel ✅
**Template:** Demon (official - noted as Grigori/Watcher)  
**File:** `Gadreel_Fallen_Angel.json`  
**Type:** Grigori (Watcher), fallen angel  
**Key Features:**
- 10,000+ years old
- Taught humanity weapons and cosmetics
- Speaks ALL human languages
- Immune to mental/emotional powers
- Firearms 6, Melee 7
- Fortitude 5, but CANNOT activate full Potence/Celerity (fallen status)
- Operates as "Gad Reilly" - master smith

### 9. The Tupilaq - Construct ✅
**Template:** Supernatural Entity (custom)  
**File:** `The_Tupilaq_Construct.json`  
**Type:** Inuit revenge construct  
**Key Features:**
- Created 1968 by dying Inuit shaman
- Made of bones, sinews, child skull
- Hunts family of USAF Captain Carrick
- Can sense targets anywhere on Earth
- 11 health levels, immune to bashing
- Soaks all damage with Stamina 6
- Bite: Strength +1 aggravated to supernaturals
- Nearly unstoppable

### 10. Azarakh Brain Bug - Spirit Parasite ✅
**Template:** Supernatural Entity (custom)  
**File:** `Azarakh_Brain_Bug.json`  
**Type:** Parasitic spirit  
**Key Features:**
- Spider-like, housecat-sized
- Invisible (Obfuscate 3 equivalent)
- Possesses mortals (10 dice vs Willpower)
- Once per day forces vile act (costs host 1 Willpower)
- Full control when host's Willpower depleted
- Telepathic network with other azarakhs
- 3 health levels in physical form

### 11. The Ifrit - Jinn ✅
**Template:** Supernatural Entity (custom)  
**File:** `The_Ifrit_Jinn.json`  
**Type:** Infernal Jinn death spirit  
**Key Features:**
- Takes form of murder victim (US Lieutenant)
- Hunts murderers (platoon members)
- Essence Pool 20, regenerates ALL at nightfall
- Heals all damage at nightfall
- Immune to fire, mind control, emotions
- Equivalent: Auspex 2, Celerity 1, Fortitude 3, Potence 3, Thaumaturgy 3
- Vulnerable to religious banishment (Muslim/Christian/Jewish faithful)

## Special Notes

### Katherine Zantosa (Revenant)
- Uses ghoul template but is NOT bonded
- `blood_bond_stage: 0` is critical
- `creature_type: "Revenant"` in custom_data
- Has vampiric disciplines through bloodline, not Embrace

### Urban Spirit (Spirit, not Werewolf)
- Uses werewolf template for database compatibility
- Extensive notes in `custom_data` explaining it's a spirit
- Uses Essence instead of Rage/Gnosis
- Cannot shapeshift like werewolf

### Gadreel (Grigori, not typical Demon)
- Uses demon template but is a Watcher (fallen angel)
- Predates demon houses
- Different from Hell-aligned demons
- Notes in biography and custom_data explain difference

### Supernatural Entity Template
- Created for unique creatures that don't fit other templates
- Flexible power system
- Supports custom immunities/vulnerabilities
- Can handle constructs, spirits, jinn, elementals, etc.

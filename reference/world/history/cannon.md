# Valley by Night: Canon History

## The Princes of Phoenix

### Prince Elijah Crenshaw
- **Clan**: Brujah
- **Title**: Prince of Phoenix (until 1973)
- **Status**: Staked and hidden in the Superstition Mountains.
- **History**: Ruled Phoenix until 1973. His reign ended when his childe, Solomon Reaves, hired an Assamite assassin to "remove" him from power.

### Prince Solomon Reaves
- **Clan**: Brujah
- **Sire**: Elijah Crenshaw
- **Title**: Prince of Phoenix (1973–1994)
- **Status**: Assassinated on the night the chronicle begins (1994).
- **History**: After hiring an Assamite to remove his sire in 1973, he assumed the throne. He ruled for over 20 years, bringing in Roland Cross as Sheriff in 1989. His reign ends on the first night of the game when he is assassinated at Hawthorn Estate.

## Key Historical Events

### 1973: The Staking of Elijah
Solomon Reaves hired an Assamite to "remove" Elijah Crenshaw. Due to a literal interpretation of the contract, the Assamite staked Elijah and hid his body in a cave in the Superstition Mountains rather than killing him. Kerry, a Gangrel drifter, witnessed this event. Solomon Reaves assumed the Princedom, believing the "removal" was final.

### 1994: The Assassination of Solomon Reaves
C.W. Whitford, the Ventrue Primogen, hired the same Assamite assassin to kill Solomon Reaves on the night of the chronicle's start. This assassination triggers the power vacuum and intrigue that drives the game's beginning.

### The Assamite Irony
The same Assamite assassin was responsible for both major shifts in Phoenix's leadership: the 1973 removal of Elijah Crenshaw and the 1994 assassination of Solomon Reaves.

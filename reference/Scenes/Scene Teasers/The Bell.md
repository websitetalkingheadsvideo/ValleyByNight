🎞️ Ending Beat: “The Bell”

The serpent coils forward.

The spotlight dies.

BLACK.

A single sound cuts through the silence—

A bell rings.

Low.
Bronze.
Funereal.

Not a club bell.
Not an alarm.

A church bell — distant, ancient, inevitable.

The sound reverberates once… twice… then fades into a deep, hollow echo.

[INT. – UNKNOWN ANTECHAMBER – NIGHT]

A door opens.

Not slammed.
Not forced.

Opened with purpose.

Pale fog rolls in, thick and deliberate, spilling across the floor like something summoned rather than drifting. It curls around unseen thresholds, swallowing edges, devouring certainty.

From within the fog—

A foot steps forward.

A woman’s foot.
Pale skin.
A high-heeled shoe, elegant, predatory, clicking softly as it meets the floor.

The sound is intimate. Final.

The fog continues to pour out behind her, obscuring everything else she might be.

We do not see her face.

We don’t need to.

Somewhere, unseen, Sarah Hansen inhales sharply.

The bell rings once more.

[CUT TO BLACK]

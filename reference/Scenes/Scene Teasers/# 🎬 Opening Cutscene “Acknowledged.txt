# 🎬 Opening Cutscene: “Acknowledged”
## (PC’s First Night in Elysium)

---

**[INT. ELYSIUM – OLD THEATER – NIGHT]**

A long-abandoned art-deco theater, reborn under dim amber chandeliers and velvet shadows. Murmured conversations ripple through the hushed court, punctuated by the soft scrape of shoes against polished marble. Smoke curls lazily under the blue-black lights overhead, drifting around statues half-lost to time.

At the center of it all, the PRINCE sits unmoving on a raised dais—surrounded by Primogen, Harpies, and other predators cloaked in silk and stillness.

A SENESCHAL/Herald steps forward.

---

## Cinematic Description

The camera glides from Kindred to Kindred: Toreador whispering behind painted fans, Ventrue appraising the room like a ledger, a lone Gangrel slouched against a column with desert dust still clinging to their boots.

The SENESCHAL lifts a polished cane and taps the marble floor once—  
**the room falls silent.**

A warm yet oppressive Desert Amber spotlight frames the stage.

---

## Character Action & Dialogue

> **Seneschal (Herald):**  
> “Honored Kindred of Phoenix… the court will now attend.”  

The SENESCHAL’s gaze finds the PC in the crowd.

> **Seneschal:**  
> “A childe comes forward to stand before Prince [PRINCE NAME] and the Camarilla.  
>  Tonight, the Accounting ends.”

A subtle shift of bodies—elders watching, testing, weighing.

The SENESCHAL gestures sharply.

> **Seneschal:**  
> “You. Step into the light.”

The camera follows the PC up the aisle, bathed in Noir Blue-Black shadows. As they ascend the steps, the packed Elysium becomes quiet enough to hear the flicker of a candle… or the scrape of a fang behind a thoughtful smile.

The PC kneels.

---

## **[CUT TO: PRINCE – CLOSE UP]**

The PRINCE rises, lit in narrow gold light like a figure in a final act onstage.

> **Prince [PRINCE NAME]:**  
> “By my right of domain, and before all gathered,  
>  I acknowledge you, [PC NAME], as one of the Camarilla.”

A slow pan across the Primogen Council—some nodding, some indifferent, some calculating.

> **Prince:**  
> “The Accounting ends tonight.  
>  You are no longer burden of sire, but citizen of this city.”

A soft exhale passes through the room—envy from the young, interest from the old.

> **Prince:**  
> “You will honor the Masquerade.  
>  You will respect Hospitality.  
>  You will keep your place among the Damned.”  

The Prince’s voice lowers:

> **Prince:**  
> “In return, the Camarilla will judge you as one of us…  
>  and protect you—when you deserve it.”

---

## **[CUT TO: HARPY – SIDE ANGLE]**

The HARPY tilts their head, amused, their voice a purr.

> **Harpy:**  
> “Recorded and remembered.  
>  Phoenix receives its newest Kindred.”

A ripple through the room—some thrilled, others irritated, one or two suddenly calculating new angles.

---

## **[CUT TO: PRINCE – WIDE SHOT]**

> **Prince:**  
> “Rise, neonate…  
>  and remember: everything you gain tonight  
>  can be taken away tomorrow.”

The Prince steps back into his throne as the lights of Elysium shift subtly, returning the murmured conversations and watchful gazes of the gathered court.

**The ceremony is complete.**

---

# End of Scene

# Valley by Night — Flashback Scenes Collection

This document consolidates all flashback and cinematic scenes developed in this conversation, in canonical order.  
Tone reference: *Angel* / *Forever Knight*.  
All scenes are observer-driven, consequence-focused, and designed to reinforce internal tensions and unlockable character logic.

---

## Flashback — “The Long Way In” (Angel-style)

**[EXT. DESERT ACCESS ROAD – NIGHT – MID-1970s]**

A mid-’70s high-end pickup truck sits at an angle on a dirt turnout — oversized hood, chrome dulled by dust, engine idling. AM radio murmurs faintly through the open window, nearly swallowed by the wind.

Rey Gonzalez is behind the wheel. Leather jacket. Hands tight on the steering wheel.

Twenty feet away, Roland Cross stands in the desert like he belongs there — tailored coat, untouched by grit. Further out, Naomi Blackbird moves slowly along a ridge, half-seen in moonlight.

Rey kills the radio.

> **Rey:** “That’s as far as the truck goes.”

> **Roland:** “You said that already.”

> **Rey:** “Because it keeps being true. Sabbat trails one way. Garou the other. My people don’t come back from places like this.”

Naomi stops.

> **Naomi:** “They’ve been watching since sundown.”

Roland doesn’t react.

> **Roland:** “And yet we still need the drop.”

Rey leans out the window.

> **Rey:** “You want more crates, more drivers — but you’re not the one explaining why somebody doesn’t come home.”

A distant howl rolls across the flats.

> **Naomi:** “That wasn’t an animal.”

> **Rey:** “No. That’s my point.”

Roland rests a hand on the truck door.

> **Roland:** “If we pull back, the Sabbat fortifies.”

> **Rey:** “And if I go forward, the ground fills with crosses.”

Silence.

> **Rey:** “Half supplies. Smaller team. No repeat runs.”

Roland hesitates — just enough.

> **Roland:** “Very well.”

The truck pulls away, headlights carving through dust.

> **Naomi:** “He won’t forget.”

> **Roland:** “Neither will I.”

## Present-Day Echo — “Close Enough to Bleed”

**[EXT. APACHE JUNCTION – TRAILER PARK PERIMETER – NIGHT – 1994]**

Chain-link fence rattling. Porch lights buzzing. Families inside thin walls.

Roland surveys the park. Rey studies access roads. Naomi circles.

> **Roland:** “We need a cache here.”

> **Rey:** “No. It’s a trailer park.”

> **Roland:** “Low scrutiny.”

> **Rey:** “Kids.”

> **Naomi:** “Blood Brothers. They hunt in pairs.”

Rey turns.

> **Rey:** “When this goes bad, it goes bad through people.”

> **Roland:** “We’re already committed.”

> **Rey:** “You are. I’m not.”

Naomi returns.

> **Naomi:** “They’re aware of us.”

A porch light flicks on.

> **Rey:** “Two miles out. Harder access. You want closer — do it without my network.”

Roland nods.

> **Roland:** “Very well.”

Something moves beneath the trailers.

Waiting.

**[CUT TO BLACK]**

## Flashback — “Why I Stay”

**[EXT. DESERT SERVICE ROAD – NIGHT – EARLY 1980s]**

No vehicles. Just moonlight and distance.

Roland walks ahead. Rey follows. Naomi ranges wide.

> **Rey:** “You push too far.”

> **Roland:** “I push until it works.”

> **Rey:** “You push until someone else pays.”

Roland stops.

> **Roland:** “I pay for it.”

> **Rey:** “You don’t bleed the way they do.”

Naomi pauses.

> **Naomi:** “He’s not wrong.”

Roland lowers his voice.

> **Roland:** “If I don’t take the risk, someone worse will.”

A distant movement. Heavy.

> **Naomi:** “There are worse leaders.”

> **Rey:** “That’s not a compliment.”

> **Roland:** “No. But it’s accurate.”

> **Rey:** “That’s why I stay. Not because you’re right. Because you know what you’re doing when you’re wrong.”

They walk on.

**[FADE OUT]**

## Flashback — “The Offer”

**[INT. ELYSIUM – MAIN HALL – NIGHT – TWO MONTHS BEFORE GAME]**

Music hums. Politics everywhere.

Rey listens as Barry Horowitz speaks, animated. Naomi circles the room, then glances once toward them.

On the balcony, a cigarette flares.

Roland Cross stands in shadow, cowboy hat brim hiding his eyes.

Naomi joins Rey and Barry. Conversation tightens.

Rey looks up. Roland nods.

Rey extends his hand. Barry shakes it, surprised. Naomi follows.

Roland turns away, slips through a side door.

Rey gestures. Barry and Naomi follow.

---

**[INT. SMALL MEETING ROOM – CONTINUOUS]**

Couches. Coffee table. No windows.

Roland stands. The others sit.

> **Roland:** “We’ve decided to make you an offer, Barry.”

The door closes most of the way.

We don’t hear the rest.

**[FADE OUT]**

## Flashback — “Barry Notices” (Barry POV)

**[INT. ELYSIUM – MAIN HALL – NIGHT]**

Barry knows he’s talking too much. Rey is listening anyway.

Naomi glances toward him. Clinical.

She joins them. Barry reins himself in.

Rey’s eyes lift. Barry follows.

A cigarette glows on the balcony.

Barry looks away first.

Minutes pass.

Roland nods.

Rey extends his hand.

Barry shakes it, pulse quickening.

Naomi’s grip is firm. Appraising.

---

**[INT. SMALL MEETING ROOM]**

Roland stands. Hat still on.

> **Roland:** “We’ve decided to make you an offer, Barry.”

Barry realizes this isn’t about ability.

It’s about cost.

He listens.

**[FADE OUT]**

## Playable Character Intro — Barry Horowitz  
### “You Were Paying Attention”

**[INT. UNMARKED ROOM – NIGHT – 1994]**

Barry waits correctly.

Roland enters. Does not sit.

> **Roland:** “You noticed something.”

> **Barry:** “What didn’t happen.”

> **Roland:** “Rey trusts you.”

> **Barry:** “He trusts I won’t make things worse.”

Roland removes his hat, sets it on the table.

> **Roland:** “This isn’t an offer.”

A folder slides across the table.

> **Roland:** “You stop being observed. You start being trusted.”

Barry opens the folder. We don’t see inside.

> **Barry:** “When do we start?”

> **Roland:** “We already have.”

Roland leaves.

Barry stands.

**[CUT TO BLACK]**

**BARRY HOROWITZ**  
*“Some people survive by force. Others by paying attention.”*

## 🎞️ Scene Card: *The Digital Underground*

**Setting:**
Resistance cell safe house in Phoenix, late night. Phreak's workspace is a digital command center—monitors cascading with code, keyboards layered with tech-modified clothing hiding devices, neural interface pulsing with connections to the digital world. The air is thick with the scent of ozone, old coffee, and something else: the electric thrill of hacking.

---

### **Scene Description (for players):**

The safe house feels *alive*—not in the way a normal building feels alive, but in the way a data center feels alive. Cool blue light from multiple monitors paints everything in phosphor glow. Phreak hunches over keyboards, fingers flying faster than thought, code cascading down screens like digital waterfalls.

This isn't just hacking. This is *art*—quantum encryption breaking, deepfake generation, network topology mapping, zero-day exploits being crafted with the precision of a surgeon and the speed of lightning. Phreak's technical specialties make him one of the most dangerous hackers in the underground, his abilities extending beyond mere code into something that might be supernatural.

His unusual ability to "speak" to AI systems—treating them almost as living entities, having philosophical conversations with them—suggests something deeper than skill. When he focuses on a system, he doesn't just hack it; he *connects* to it, touching something that might be awareness, might be something else entirely.

But beneath the cocky hacker exterior lies someone who's been running for so long, he's forgotten who he was before becoming Phreak. He lost someone important to corporate surveillance, though the details remain unclear, and built a reputation in hacker circles before joining Core's resistance.

His relationship with Core is deep respect mixed with friendly antagonism, using humor to deflect deeper feelings he won't acknowledge. Together, they're a force: Phreak's digital skills and Core's tactical knowledge creating a partnership that could dismantle the surveillance state.

But Phreak's impulsiveness and recklessness make him vulnerable. His overconfidence in digital abilities can blind him to physical danger, and his tendency to treat resistance as an exciting game can put others at risk. When the corporations strike back, when the systems fight back, Phreak will have to confront real consequences and stakes—or lose everything he's fighting for.

---

### **GM Notes:**

* **Disciplines in play:** Phreak is listed as Nosferatu (based on cybernetic modifications), suggesting supernatural abilities that extend beyond mere hacking. His neural interface port and ability to communicate with AI systems suggest Auspex or Obfuscate abilities, possibly combined with technological enhancements.

* **Tone:** Cocky, brilliant, vulnerable. Phreak approaches hacking like an art form—exciting, thrilling, almost a game. But beneath the humor lies someone who's running from his past, fighting for a cause he believes in, and struggling with the weight of real consequences.

* **First impression:** Players see a hacker who's both brilliant and reckless—a digital prodigy whose abilities extend beyond normal hacking into something that might be supernatural. His partnership with Core makes him valuable, but his impulsiveness makes him dangerous.

* **Hooks:**
  * Phreak's past catches up with him—former colleagues hunting him, systems he hacked seeking revenge, creating a race against digital discovery.
  * He must choose between digital safety and physical danger—hacking operations that require exposing himself, risking capture to save others.
  * His relationship with Core becomes complicated—romantic tension threatens to disrupt the partnership, or external pressure forces difficult choices.
  * Discover that the AI systems Phreak communicates with are more aware than he realizes—possibly sentient, possibly dangerous, possibly allies in ways he doesn't understand.
  * Use Phreak's hacking skills to infiltrate corporate systems—his digital abilities make him invaluable, but using them risks discovery and retaliation.

* **Plot foreshadowing:** "Phreak talks to the AI systems like they're alive. Maybe they are. And when the digital underground becomes a battlefield, his connections might be the key to victory—or the path to destruction."

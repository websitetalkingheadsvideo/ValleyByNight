# Sarah Hansen

**Clan/Bloodline:** Daughter of Cacophony (Clanless)  
**Generation:** 9th  
**Sect:** Camarilla-Aligned Independent  
**Status:** Acknowledged (Prince’s Court)  
**Chronicle:** Valley by Night  
**Location:** Mesa, Arizona  
**Role:** Nightclub Owner, Performer, Toreador Ally  

---

## Concept
Haunting chanteuse and nightclub owner whose voice carries supernatural beauty and melancholy. Sarah thrives in the liminal space between Kindred politics and mortal emotion. Her songs are whispered to influence moods, gather secrets, and reveal the pain she hides beneath the surface.

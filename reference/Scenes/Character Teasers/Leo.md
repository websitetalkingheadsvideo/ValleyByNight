## 🎞️ Scene Card: *The Invisible Hand*

**Setting:**
An underground command center along the 24th Street corridor, late night. Concrete walls, utility schematics, jury-rigged consoles. Then the utility tunnels themselves—dark, wet, filled with the infrastructure that keeps Phoenix running.

---

### **Scene Description (for players):**

Beneath Phoenix's streets, in the utility tunnels and storm drains that most Kindred never see, Leo maintains his command center. He's a gaunt Nosferatu with patchwork utility coveralls, a hard hat fitted with jury-rigged headlamps, and a bandolier of tools. His skin is mottled like wet concrete, but his eyes are bright with feral focus.

Blueprints and utility schematics cover every surface, and coax cable reels are slung over his hunched shoulders. He maps utility tunnels, taps data lines, and guides Anarch raids using traffic cameras and water-flow sensors. To keep the Anarchs safe, he plans distractions to mask haven construction and disrupt Camarilla surveillance.

Leo rarely surfaces, and when he does, it's only to splice a line or escort neonates through storm drains. But down here, in the darkness beneath Phoenix, he's the invisible hand—the one who controls the flow of information, the one who keeps the underground alive.

---

### **GM Notes:**

* **Disciplines in play:** Leo's *Obfuscate* helps him move unseen through the underground, while his *Animalism* might allow him to communicate with rats and other underground creatures. His technical expertise with utility systems and data networks makes him invaluable for surveillance and sabotage operations.
* **Tone:** Feral, focused, with an underlying sense of isolation. Leo is a survivor who's found his purpose in the underground, and he's very good at what he does. The contrast between the surface world and his underground domain creates a sense of separation, of someone who's chosen to live in the shadows.
* **First impression:** Players see a Nosferatu who seems perfectly at home in the darkness, a technical expert who's built his own command center in the sewers. But there's something *off*—the way he rarely surfaces, the way he's become part of the infrastructure itself, the way he's the invisible hand that guides operations without being seen.
* **Hooks:**
  * Help set up a new sewer haven under Camarilla noses—Leo needs assistance with construction, security, or masking the operation from surveillance.
  * Defend Leo's data node from Sabbat saboteurs—someone is trying to destroy his command center, and he needs help protecting it.
  * Bargain for access to the Prince's fiber taps—Leo has information that could be valuable, but he'll only share it for the right price.
  * A utility tunnel collapse traps Leo—PCs might need to rescue him, or help him navigate the damaged infrastructure.
  * Someone is tracking Leo's movements through the utility systems—he needs help identifying and stopping the surveillance before it exposes his operations.
  * Leo discovers something dangerous in the underground—maybe a Sabbat cell, a Garou den, or something else that threatens the Anarchs.
* **Plot foreshadowing:** "Beneath Phoenix's streets, every line is a weapon, and every tunnel is a path—but even the invisible hand can be caught when someone knows where to look."

# Cinematic Intro – “Ledger of the Dead” (Revised Cut)

## *Full Scene + VO Captions*

## SCENE 1 — EXTERIOR: SCOTTSDALE PRIVATE BANK (NIGHT)
**Wide shot:** A brushed-metal sign gleams under parking-lot sodium lights.  
A black Mercedes idles. Alessandro steps out—perfect velvet blazer, gold chain catching the light.

**VO:** *“Some debts never die. Some you inherit.”*

---

## SCENE 2 — INTERIOR LOBBY: AFTER HOURS
Fluorescent lights buzz. The branch manager, dressed just a touch too provocatively, greets him.

**MANAGER:** “Mr. Vescari… right this way.”

**VO:** *“Giovanni wealth runs deep. Deeper than vaults. Deeper than graves.”*

Security monitors flicker as they walk.

---

## SCENE 3 — VAULT CORRIDOR
The lights stutter. The temperature drops.  
Her breath fogs. Alessandro’s does not.

**VO:** *“Ghosts gather where promises were broken.”*

---

## SCENE 4 — INSIDE THE VAULT
Rows of deposit boxes like metal ossuary drawers.  
Alessandro opens his family box. Inside: a frost‑rimmed necromantic tome.

Whispers hiss. Papers rustle. Shadows stretch.

The manager instinctively turns to leave.

**ALESSANDRO:** “You’ve had time to reconsider.”

**MANAGER (shaking):** “I… I was just—”

**ALESSANDRO (Dominate):** “Freeze.”

She obeys instantly.

**VO:** *“Control the living. Negotiate with the dead.”*

---

## SCENE 5 — NEGOTIATION WITH THE WRAITH
Pages flutter violently. Frost creeps along the walls.

A skeletal handprint appears.

**WHISPER:** *“…you should not have returned…”*

**ALESSANDRO:** “You owe the family an answer.”

The tome twists, resisting.

**ALESSANDRO:** “Speak.”

**WRAITH:** *“…the Setites aren’t who you should fear… it’s who watches them…”*

---

## SCENE 6 — THE SILENCING
The tome slams shut. Alessandro brushes frost off his sleeves.

Turning back to the manager:

**ALESSANDRO:** “Forget this.”

She softens—confused but calm.

**MANAGER:** “Was there… something? Sorry, I spaced out.”

**ALESSANDRO:** “All handled.”

---

## SCENE 7 — FINAL SHOT (INSIDE THE BOX)
**POV from inside the deposit box.**  
Alessandro slides the writhing tome back in.

For a split second:  
A pale face presses against the pages, screaming silently.

The lid closes.  
Darkness.

**VO:** *“Some ledgers balance themselves. Others demand blood.”*

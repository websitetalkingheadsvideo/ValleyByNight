**Mood:** The jester revealed

**Description:**
Misfortune sits hunched over a desk made of stacked stage props. His motley is crimson and black, bells glinting. He hums quietly.

**Description:**
He presses the Harpy’s seal onto fresh parchment. Wax glows warm. He lifts the page and pins it to the wall with unnerving care.

# Sarah Hansen — Cinematic Introduction
## Valley by Night Character Introduction

**Character:** Sarah Hansen, Toreador vocalist  
**Duration:** 45 seconds  
**Shots:** 8  
**VO Lines:** 6  
**Setting:** The Velvet Door, Mesa — Club stage, night. Audience dark. One person watching.

---

## SCENE HEADER

**INT. THE VELVET DOOR — MAIN ROOM — NIGHT**

**TIME:** Late, October 1994. Last set of the evening.

---

## SHOT 1: ESTABLISHING SHOT (WIDE) — 5 seconds

**CAMERA:** 35mm lens, slow push-in from the back of the house

The stage glows in a single warm amber spotlight. Beyond it, the audience floor is pitch black — tables and chairs swallowed by shadow. Velvet curtains frame the stage. A chrome microphone waits center. The rest of the club is invisible. One silhouette sits in the darkness, far back, barely a shape.

**LIGHTING:** Single warm amber spotlight on stage; audience area unlit; crimson neon accent from the bar sign bleeding at frame edge; teal moonlight through high windows (diffused)

**AUDIO:** Piano sustain, fading; distant city hum; silence settling; one slow breath

**VO (Sarah Hansen, restrained, almost detached):**
*"They call it passion. But passion's just hunger with better lighting."*

---

## SHOT 2: CHARACTER REVEAL (MCU) — 6 seconds

**CAMERA:** 50mm lens, slow dolly toward stage

**SARAH HANSEN** stands at the microphone. Copper-red hair, long and softly waved, catches the spotlight like embers. Pale skin luminous against a wine-red dress—body-skimming, elegant. Her eyes are half-closed. One hand rests lightly on the mic stand; the other opens subtly, palm outward—that unconscious gesture of invitation. She holds the last note, then releases. Silence.

**LIGHTING:** Warm amber key from above; minimal rim; face sharp, body fading into shadow; no fill from the dark house

**AUDIO:** Final note decays; silence; faint AC hum; her breath

**VO (Sarah, continuing):**
*"Beauty isn't the lie. It's the promise that someone's still watching."*

---

## SHOT 3: INSERT — MICROPHONE (CLOSE-UP) — 4 seconds

**CAMERA:** 85mm lens, extreme close-up

Chrome ribbed microphone. Her fingers rest beside it—precise, controlled. A single spotlight reflects in the metal. Beyond, her dress blurs into deep crimson shadow. The mic is the only anchor. The stage, the club, the darkness—all reduced to this.

**LIGHTING:** Warm amber reflection on chrome; shallow depth of field; crimson bleed in bokeh

**AUDIO:** Near silence; faint electrical hum from the mic; one heartbeat of stillness

**VO (Sarah, quieter):**
*"One audience. One witness. That's enough."*

---

## SHOT 4: EMOTIONAL BEAT (MCU) — 7 seconds

**CAMERA:** 50mm lens, slow push-in on her face

Sarah opens her eyes. She looks out into the dark—not at the camera, but past it, toward where the single watcher sits. Her gaze is composed, unreadable. Lips precise. No smile. She knows exactly where they are. Serene. Almost detached. As if the emotion passes through her rather than from her.

**LIGHTING:** Warm amber key at 35°; cool teal fill from the dark house; eyes sharp, rest of face in gentle shadow

**AUDIO:** Silence; then her voice, barely above a whisper

**VO (Sarah, measured):**
*"The room is full when it's empty. You learn to feel the one who matters."*

---

## SHOT 5: SECONDARY ACTION — POV REVERSE (MEDIUM) — 6 seconds

**CAMERA:** 50mm lens, from the audience—the watcher's POV

The stage from the darkness. Sarah is a silhouette against the amber glow, backlit now. She tilts her head slightly. A controlled gesture. The spotlight narrows. The club feels smaller. Colder. She is performing for one person. The rest of the world has already left.

**LIGHTING:** Backlit stage; silhouette of Sarah; amber halo; audience POV in total shadow

**AUDIO:** Her breath, amplified by the silence; distant traffic; the hum of neon

**VO (Sarah, intimate):**
*"They remember the song. Never the singer. Once the lights go out."*

---

## SHOT 6: SUPERNATURAL SUBTLE MOMENT (MCU) — 5 seconds

**CAMERA:** 85mm lens, extreme close-up on her eyes

Sarah's gaze holds. For a moment, the pupils dilate—too fast, too precise. The warm bloom of Presence. The spotlight seems to bend toward her. The silence deepens. She is not just a singer. She is Toreador. Control. Not just beauty. The watcher in the dark is already caught.

**LIGHTING:** Warm bloom intensifies; amber catches inhuman reflection in her eyes; teal shadow recedes

**AUDIO:** Silence—city sounds drop to near-zero; one held breath

**VO (Sarah, voice drops, predatory undertone):**
*"Stay. The stage isn't done with you yet."*

---

## SHOT 7: CLOSING SHOT (WIDE) — 7 seconds

**CAMERA:** 35mm lens, slow pull-back

Sarah stands alone at the microphone. The spotlight holds her. The audience floor remains black. One figure, barely visible, sits in the shadows. She bows her head. Eyes close. The last gesture—economical, intentional. The velvet curtains breathe. The crimson neon pulses. She is mesmerizing. She is distant. Something essential held just out of reach.

**LIGHTING:** Single amber spotlight; audience in darkness; crimson neon accent; teal moonlight through high windows; shallow depth of field

**AUDIO:** Piano note, single, sustained; fade to silence; neon buzz; door creak in the distance

**VO (Sarah, final, elegiac):**
*"Beauty is the promise. I am the proof."*

---

## SHOT 8: FADE TO BLACK — 5 seconds

**CAMERA:** Fade to black

The spotlight dims. The stage goes dark. The single watcher vanishes into shadow.

**AUDIO:** Music swells slightly (slow jazz, low strings), then fades

**TITLE CARD APPEARS:**
*"VALLEY BY NIGHT"*
*"SARAH HANSEN"*
*"Toreador Vocalist"*

## TECHNICAL NOTES

**Color Grade:**
- Soft vignette
- Slight film grain
- Enhanced noir contrast
- Warm/cool split tones (amber/teal)
- Subtle haze

**Camera Movement:**
- Slow, deliberate
- No handheld
- Smooth dolly and push-ins
- Depth of field: shallow to medium

**Toreador Motifs:**
- Microphone as focal object
- Eyes closed or half-closed during performance
- Deep red fabric against neutral, shadowed backgrounds
- Soft spotlight from above, minimal rim light
- Hands used sparingly, never flailing

**Disciplines (Subtle):**
- Presence: Warm bloom in shot 6; the sense that the watcher is compelled to stay
- Auspex 1: She "feels" the one who matters in the dark

**GM Notes:**
- Audience is dark; only one person visible—PC, elder, stalker, or chronicle-specific NPC
- Establishes Sarah as controlled, deliberate, emotionally distant even in performance
- The single watcher creates intimacy and unease—who are they? Why are they alone?
- Her club, her stage, her rules
- The emptiness emphasizes her isolation and her choice to perform for one

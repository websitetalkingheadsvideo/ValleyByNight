# WoD Master Booklist

This document is a comprehensive listing of all books ever published for the World of Darkness setting and its various game lines. Compiled as the World of Darkness was coming to an end, it represents 13 years' and several game lines' worth of supplements, all listed alphabetically for reference. The list includes stock numbers and ISBNs to help track down elusive titles.

## Purpose and Scope

- This is a reference document listing all World of Darkness books.
- It covers all game lines in the World of Darkness.
- Books are listed alphabetically with stock numbers and ISBNs.
- Serves as a comprehensive catalog of World of Darkness publications.

## Game Lines Covered

The booklist covers all major World of Darkness game lines, including:
- **Vampire: The Masquerade**: The original World of Darkness game
- **Werewolf: The Apocalypse**: The game of shapeshifting warriors
- **Mage: The Ascension**: The game of modern magic
- **Wraith: The Oblivion**: The game of the restless dead
- **Changeling: The Dreaming**: The game of fae souls in mortal bodies
- **Hunter: The Reckoning**: The game of mortals fighting monsters
- **Mummy: The Resurrection**: The game of resurrected ancient souls
- **Mind's Eye Theatre**: Live-action versions of the above games
- Various supplements, sourcebooks, and related materials

## Organization

The booklist is organized alphabetically, making it easy to find specific titles. Each entry includes:
- Book title
- Stock number
- ISBN number

## Key Themes

- **Comprehensive Reference**: Complete listing of all World of Darkness publications.
- **Historical Documentation**: Records 13 years of game publishing history.
- **Resource for Collectors**: Helps track down specific books with stock numbers and ISBNs.

## Purpose and Value

WoD Master Booklist serves as:
- A comprehensive reference for all World of Darkness books
- A historical document of White Wolf's publishing history
- A resource for collectors seeking specific titles
- A catalog with stock numbers and ISBNs for tracking books

This document is useful for collectors, researchers, and anyone interested in the complete publishing history of the World of Darkness, providing a comprehensive alphabetical listing of all books with their identifying numbers.

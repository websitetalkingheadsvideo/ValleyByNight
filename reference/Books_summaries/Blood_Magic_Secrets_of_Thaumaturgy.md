# Blood Magic - Secrets of Thaumaturgy

This supplement for Vampire: The Masquerade and Mind's Eye Theatre explores the dark art of Thaumaturgy - the blood magic practiced primarily by Clan Tremere. The book provides extensive new paths, rituals, and systems for blood magic, along with information on learning Thaumaturgy, creating new powers, and the various traditions of blood sorcery practiced by different clans.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book expands upon the basic Thaumaturgy rules from the core rulebooks.
- It provides new paths and rituals for Tremere and other blood magicians.
- Explores the development and philosophy of Thaumaturgy.
- Includes information on non-Tremere blood magic traditions (Setite Sorcery, Assamite Sorcery, Koldunic Sorcery).
- Provides rules for creating new paths and rituals.

## Introduction: Meanwhile Back in the Real World

The book opens with an important disclaimer: This is a work of fiction and a supplement to a storytelling game. The practice of Thaumaturgy has spawned an enormous body of specialized language, and the book establishes common ground for those terms while noting that colloquially words may acquire different meanings.

## The Development of Thaumaturgy

### To Seek Immortality

Thaumaturgy was developed relatively recently in Kindred terms by Clan Tremere. The Warlocks were originally mortal mages who sought immortality through blood magic, eventually transforming themselves into vampires. This act earned them the undying enmity of the Tzimisce, whose elder did not survive the research.

### The Obsession

Magi have spent centuries delving into the mysteries of Thaumaturgy. Even in modern nights, Kindred blood magicians devote vast amounts of time and resources to researching ancient texts and creating new sorceries. This obsession may seem odd to an outsider, but near-blinding ambition and survival motivated the members of Clan Tremere from the beginning.

## Paths: The Power of the Will

### Path Structure

Thaumaturgy consists of paths - codified sequences of magic powers united by a shared concept. Path magic usually requires little or no preparation or ingredients, unlike rituals which require time, components, and incantations.

### New Paths Presented

The book includes numerous new Thaumaturgy paths:

- **Path of Blood**: Advanced manipulation of vitae
- **Lure of Flames**: Control and creation of fire
- **Path of Conjuring**: Summoning and binding spirits
- **Movement of the Mind**: Telekinesis and psychokinesis
- **Path of Technomancy**: Control over technology and machines
- **Path of Weather Control**: Manipulation of weather patterns
- **Path of Corruption**: Twisting and perverting targets
- **Path of Spirit Manipulation**: Control over spirits and wraiths
- **Path of Mars**: Combat-focused magic
- **Path of the Focused Mind**: Mental discipline and clarity
- **Path of Alchemy**: Creation of magical potions and elixirs

### Path Mechanics

- Most paths use Willpower rolls for activation.
- Each path has five levels of power.
- Blood Trait expenditure is always required when activating a thaumaturgical power.
- Paths can be learned from teachers, grimoires, or through research.

## Rituals

### Ritual Structure

Rituals are sequences of actions that produce magical effects. Unlike paths, rituals require:
- Time to cast (ranging from minutes to hours)
- Physical components (ingredients, tools, materials)
- Incantations (words, chants, or prayers)
- Intelligence + Occult rolls

### Ritual Levels

Rituals are organized by level (1-5), with higher level rituals being more powerful but requiring more time, resources, and skill to perform.

### New Rituals Presented

The book includes extensive lists of new rituals organized by level:

**Level One Rituals**: Basic protective and utility spells
**Level Two Rituals**: More complex effects requiring specific ingredients
**Level Three Rituals**: Powerful magic with significant requirements
**Level Four Rituals**: Extremely potent effects
**Level Five Rituals**: Legendary magic of immense power

## Learning Thaumaturgy

### Finding a Teacher

Most thaumaturges learn from teachers, who demand payment in return for lessons. This may take the form of:
- Cash
- Boons
- Intimate services
- Hunting for the master
- Performing domestic chores
- Fetching rare components
- Accepting a blood bond or other supernatural compulsion

### Finding Grimoires

Thaumaturges can seek rare and hidden texts that reveal secrets of blood magic:
- Tombs of ancient blood sorcerers
- Hidden temples with carved instructions
- Elite auctions of art and antiquities
- Secret libraries

### Research

Characters can try to research their own paths and rituals based on legends and theories of their occult tradition. This takes years or even decades of experimentation and can be dangerous when experiments fail.

### Social Barriers

The Tremere rarely teach their Hermetic style of Thaumaturgy to vampires outside their clan. Some Tremere hunt such outsiders to preserve their secrets and supremacy. Clan, ethnic, and religious barriers also exist between different traditions of blood magic.

## Non-Tremere Apprentices

The book discusses the challenges and opportunities for non-Tremere learning Thaumaturgy, including the political implications and the risks involved.

## Creating New Paths

Rules for creating entirely new Thaumaturgy paths:
- Requires minimum Occult 5 and Thaumaturgy 5
- Needs a laboratory that might also serve as a haven
- Each level requires extended Intelligence + Occult rolls (difficulty 5)
- Each roll represents a full year of research and experimentation
- Character expends five blood points per level of the power
- Rolling a botch means losing all accumulated successes

## Creating New Rituals

Rules for creating new rituals:
- Character needs Occult higher than the ritual level being created
- Must research the ritual through appropriate sources
- Extended action roll: Intelligence + Occult (difficulty = ritual's level, max 10)
- Must accumulate three times the ritual's level in successes
- Each increment takes time based on the ritual's level
- Character must expend two blood points per level of the ritual

## Storyteller Options

Optional rules for Storytellers who want to make thaumaturgical power easier or harder to come by:
- Optional experience cost for rituals
- Adjusting learning times
- Modifying difficulty of research
- Handling rare ingredients and components

## Setite Sorcery

- Unique rituals and practices
- Religious and cultural aspects
- Relationship to Egyptian mythology

## Assamite Sorcery

Information on the blood magic practiced by the Assamites:
- Dur-An-Ki tradition
- Unique paths and rituals
- Cultural and religious barriers
- Relationship to Middle Eastern traditions

## Koldunic Sorcery

Brief information on the blood magic practiced by the Tzimisce:
- Elemental and nature-based magic
- Regional variations
- Relationship to the land

## Blood Magic Errata

Corrections and additions to previously published material, including the Level Five power for the Setite Sorcery path "The Snake Inside" - Temperance.

## Key Themes

- **Power Through Knowledge**: Thaumaturgy represents the ultimate expression of knowledge as power in the World of Darkness.
- **The Price of Magic**: Learning and using blood magic always comes with a cost - in time, resources, blood, or favors owed.
- **Secrecy and Monopoly**: The Tremere guard their secrets jealously, creating political tensions throughout Kindred society.
- **Tradition and Innovation**: The tension between following established paths and creating new magic.
- **Cultural Barriers**: Different blood magic traditions reflect different cultures, religions, and worldviews.

## Purpose and Value

Blood Magic - Secrets of Thaumaturgy serves as:

- An essential expansion for players wanting to explore blood magic in depth.
- A comprehensive guide to Thaumaturgy paths and rituals.
- Rules for creating new magical powers.
- Information on non-Tremere blood magic traditions.
- A resource for Storytellers running games involving blood magicians.

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play a Tremere or other blood magician, understand the complexities of Thaumaturgy, or incorporate blood magic as a major element in their chronicles.

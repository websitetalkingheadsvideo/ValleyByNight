# Clanbook: Toreador

This supplement for Vampire: The Masquerade provides comprehensive information on Clan Toreador, the "Artistes" known for their appreciation of beauty, art, and aesthetics. The book explores the clan's history, their relationship with art and beauty, their role in Camarilla society, and the division within the clan between true artists and poseurs. The book examines how Toreador navigate a world where beauty and art are both their greatest strength and their greatest weakness.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book provides detailed information on Clan Toreador for players and Storytellers.
- It explores the clan's history and their relationship with art and beauty.
- Examines the division between Artistes and Poseurs within the clan.
- Provides character creation options, new Disciplines, and information on Toreador culture.

## History

The Toreador trace their lineage to Arikel, one of the Third Generation. The clan has always been associated with beauty, art, and aesthetics. They are one of the founding clans of the Camarilla and play an important role in Camarilla society.

### Art and Beauty

The Toreador are defined by their appreciation of art and beauty. They are drawn to beautiful things, whether they are works of art, beautiful people, or beautiful experiences. This appreciation shapes every aspect of their unlife.

### The Division

The Toreador are divided between Artistes (true artists who create) and Poseurs (those who merely appreciate or pretend). This division creates tension within the clan, as Artistes often look down on Poseurs, while Poseurs may resent the Artistes' talent.

### Camarilla Society

The Toreador are one of the founding clans of the Camarilla and play an important role in Camarilla society. They often serve as Harpies, using their social skills and appreciation of beauty to maintain the Masquerade and shape Kindred society.

## Key Themes

- **Beauty**: The Toreador's appreciation of beauty in all its forms.
- **Art**: The clan's relationship with art, both as creators and appreciators.
- **The Division**: The tension between Artistes and Poseurs.
- **Camarilla Society**: The Toreador's role in maintaining and shaping Camarilla society.
- **Aesthetics**: The clan's focus on appearance, style, and presentation.

## Purpose and Value

Clanbook: Toreador serves as:
- Comprehensive information on Clan Toreador
- The history of the clan and their relationship with art and beauty
- Exploration of the division between Artistes and Poseurs
- Character creation options for Toreador characters
- New Disciplines and powers
- Information on Toreador culture and their role in Camarilla society

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play or understand Toreador characters, providing complete information on the "Artistes" and exploring how their appreciation of beauty and art has shaped them into one of the most influential clans in Camarilla society.

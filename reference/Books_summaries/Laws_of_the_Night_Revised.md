# Laws of the Night Revised

The document is the Revised Edition core rulebook for Mind's Eye Theatre Vampire: The Masquerade, providing all essential rules, systems, and information needed to play and run live-action roleplaying games in the World of Darkness. This revised edition updates and expands upon the original Laws of the Night with improved rules, expanded clan information, and refined gameplay mechanics.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book contains all the information needed to start playing and telling stories in Mind's Eye Theatre Vampire: The Masquerade.
- It is based on the tabletop game Vampire: The Masquerade, updated for live-action play.
- It provides elegantly simple rules designed to be easy to play and easier to start.
- The revised edition includes updated rules, expanded clan descriptions, and refined systems for combat, disciplines, and character advancement.

## Introduction

The book introduces Mind's Eye Theatre as a live-action roleplaying experience where players take on the personas of vampires in the World of Darkness. The game emphasizes storytelling over winning, with players creating narratives through their character actions and interactions.

### The Rules Thou Shalt Never Break

Core safety and gameplay rules that must always be followed:

- **No Touching. No Stunts**: Physical contact and stunts are forbidden to prevent injury and maintain safety.
- **No Weapons**: Real, fake, or toy weapons are prohibited. Use item cards instead.
- **No Drugs or Drinking**: Players must not be under the influence during games.
- **It's Only a Game**: Maintain perspective and leave character conflicts in-game.
- **Be Mindful of Others**: Respect non-players and maintain good relations with venues and authorities.
- **Do What Works For You**: The golden rule - adapt rules to fit your chronicle while maintaining fun.
- **Have Fun**: The goal is enjoyment, not winning.

## Chapter One: Introduction

Provides an overview of Mind's Eye Theatre, the World of Darkness setting, and the nature of vampires (Kindred). Explains the Gothic-Punk aesthetic of the setting and introduces core concepts like the Embrace, the Camarilla, and the Masquerade.

## Chapter Two: The Clans

A complete overview of all vampire clans and bloodlines available for play:

- **Camarilla Clans**: Brujah, Gangrel, Malkavian, Nosferatu, Toreador, Tremere, Ventrue
- **Independent Clans**: Assamite, Followers of Set, Giovanni, Ravnos
- **Sabbat Clans**: Lasombra, Tzimisce, and their Antitribu variants
- **Rare Bloodlines**: Various bloodlines including Salubri, Daughters of Cacophony, and others

Each clan entry includes their history, philosophy, disciplines, and role in Kindred society.

## Chapter Three: Character Creation

Complete character creation system including:

- **Attributes**: Physical, Social, and Mental traits
- **Abilities**: Talents, Skills, and Knowledges
- **Disciplines**: Supernatural powers granted by the Embrace
- **Backgrounds**: Resources, contacts, and advantages available to characters
- **Virtues**: Conscience, Conviction, and Courage
- **Archetypes**: Nature and Demeanor for personality definition

The system is designed to be quick and intuitive, allowing new players to create characters in minutes.

## Chapter Four: Disciplines

Detailed descriptions of all Disciplines available to vampires:

- **Core Disciplines**: Animalism, Auspex, Celerity, Dominate, Fortitude, Obfuscate, Potence, Presence, Protean
- **Clan-Specific Disciplines**: Blood Magic (Thaumaturgy), Chimerstry, Dementation, Necromancy, Quietus, Serpentis, Vicissitude, and others
- **Discipline Levels**: Basic, Intermediate, Advanced, Elder, Master, and Ascendent powers

Each Discipline includes descriptions of powers at various levels and their game effects.

## Chapter Five: Rules, Systems and Drama

Comprehensive rules for gameplay:

- **Basic Rules**: Core mechanics for challenges, conflicts, and resolution
- **Combat Systems**: Rules for physical confrontations and combat resolution
- **Derangements**: Mental conditions that may afflict Kindred
- **Health and Healing**: Systems for tracking damage and recovery
- **The Blood Bond**: Rules for the powerful emotional connection created by drinking another vampire's blood
- **Frenzy**: Rules for when the Beast takes control
- **Generation**: How a vampire's generation affects their power and limitations

## Chapter Six: Storytelling

Guidance for Storytellers and Narrators:

- **Creating Stories**: How to craft engaging plots and narratives
- **Running Games**: Practical advice for managing players and scenes
- **Dealing with Players**: Handling conflicts, disputes, and problem situations
- **Narrator Roles**: How Narrators assist Storytellers in running games
- **Scene Management**: Organizing and directing game sessions

## Chapter Seven: Allies and Antagonists

Information on other denizens of the World of Darkness:

- **Other Supernaturals**: Werewolves, Mages, Wraiths, and other creatures
- **Mortals**: Hunters, police, and other human threats
- **Incorporating Antagonists**: How to use these elements in chronicles
- **Cross-Genre Play**: Guidelines for games involving multiple supernatural types

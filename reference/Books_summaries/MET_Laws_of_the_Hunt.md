# MET Laws of the Hunt

This book is the core rulebook for Mind's Eye Theatre about mortals who hunt supernatural creatures. Unlike Laws of the Reckoning, which focuses on the Imbued chosen by the Heralds, Laws of the Hunt is about mortals who wage their war against the supernatural for personal reasons or on behalf of existing organizations such as the Inquisition or the FBI's Special Affairs Division.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book contains all the information needed to play mortals who hunt supernatural creatures in MET.
- It focuses on ordinary humans and organizations that fight the supernatural.
- Some hunters are ordinary humans; others are magicians, psychics, supernatural kin, or similarly strange beings.
- Provides rules for creating and playing hunter characters.

## Hunters

Information on the various types of mortals who hunt supernatural creatures:
- Ordinary humans who have discovered the supernatural
- Members of organizations like the Inquisition
- Government agents (such as the FBI's Special Affairs Division)
- Magicians and psychics who fight monsters
- Supernatural kin who hunt their own kind
- Independent hunters operating alone or in small groups

## Organizations

Information on established organizations that hunt the supernatural:
- **The Inquisition**: Religious organization fighting supernatural threats
- **Special Affairs Division**: Government agency dealing with supernatural phenomena
- Other organizations and their methods
- How organizations operate and recruit

## Character Creation

The process of creating hunter characters, including:
- Attributes and Abilities
- Backgrounds
- Organization affiliations
- Special abilities (for magicians, psychics, etc.)
- Character concepts

## Rules and Systems

The core rules system for resolving actions, conflicts, and challenges:
- Basic conflict resolution
- Combat systems
- Social interactions
- Using special abilities
- Drama systems

## Key Themes

- **Mortals vs. Monsters**: Ordinary humans fighting against supernatural threats.
- **Organization and Structure**: Many hunters work within established organizations.
- **Personal Motivation**: Hunters often have personal reasons for their war.
- **Variety of Approaches**: Different hunters use different methods and abilities.

## Purpose and Value

MET Laws of the Hunt serves as:
- The complete rulebook for playing mortal hunters in MET
- Information on organizations that fight the supernatural
- Rules for creating and playing various types of hunter characters
- A different perspective on the World of Darkness from mortal hunters

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play or run games about mortals who hunt supernatural creatures, providing all the tools needed to tell stories of ordinary humans (and some not-so-ordinary ones) fighting against the monsters of the World of Darkness.

# Reference Guide

The document is a comprehensive directory and quick-reference index for the Mind's Eye Theatre (MET) Vampire: The Masquerade system, providing page number references to locate specific information across all MET rulebooks and supplements.

## Purpose and Structure

- The Reference Guide serves as a master index for the entire MET VTM system, allowing players and Storytellers to quickly locate rules, mechanics, and lore across multiple sourcebooks.
- It is organized into logical categories covering all aspects of character creation, gameplay mechanics, and setting information.
- Each entry provides the book title and page number where detailed information can be found.

## Sects and Faction Information

The guide catalogs all major sects and their source locations:

- **Autarkis**: Referenced in The Long Night
- **Independents**: Covered in Laws of the Night
- **Anarchs**: Detailed in Laws of the Night: Anarch Guide
- **Laibon**: Information in Faith & Fire
- **Camarilla**: Comprehensive coverage in Laws of the Night: Camarilla Guide
- **Sabbat**: Extensive details in Laws of the Night: Sabbat Guide

- **Revenant Bloodlines**: Bratovitch, Grimaldi, Obertus, Oprichniki, Zantosa

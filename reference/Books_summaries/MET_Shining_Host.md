# MET The Shining Host

This book is the live-action version of Changeling: The Dreaming for Mind's Eye Theatre. Like Laws of the Night or Oblivion, this book allows you to take one of the games of the World of Darkness and transform it into a live-action experience. With this book, you can leave the table and the dice behind and step onto the stage yourself.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book contains all the information needed to play Mind's Eye Theatre Changeling: The Dreaming.
- It is based on the tabletop game Changeling: The Dreaming, adapted for live-action play.
- Focuses on changelings - fae souls born into human bodies for protection from Banality.
- Provides rules for character creation, Arts (fae magic), and Storytelling.

## The Rules of Safety

Critical safety rules that must always be followed:
- **It's Only a Game** - Don't take things too seriously.
- **No Touching** - Never actually have physical contact with other players.
- **No Stunts** - Never climb, jump, run, leap or swing during a game.
- **No Weapons** - Fake or real weapons of any sort are absolutely forbidden.
- **No Drugs or Drinking** - Impaired players are a danger to others and the game.
- **Be Mindful of Others** - Not everyone around you is playing the game.
- **The Rules are Flexible** - Feel free to ignore or adjust rules if it makes your game better (The Golden Rule).
- **Have Fun** - The goal is to tell great stories, not to achieve superiority over others.

## What is Storytelling?

The book emphasizes storytelling as one of the oldest forms of human communication. Live-action roleplaying (LARP) is presented as a return to the oldest form of participatory storytelling, exploring universal tales of the Hero's Journey.

## What is Mind's Eye Theatre?

Playing Mind's Eye Theatre is like being in a movie. You and your friends portray the main characters, but the script follows your decisions. The director of this improvisational movie is called the Storyteller, assisted by Narrators. Most scenes are played out in real-time, and always in character.

## The Character

When you play The Shining Host, you take on the persona of a changeling - a fae soul born into a human body for protection from the cold winds of disbelief and Banality. Your character can be anyone from any walk of life. The only limit on your character concept is your imagination.

Creating a character is easy and only takes a few minutes. However, your character should be more than just Traits and numbers - she should be a living, breathing personality with a past, motives, drives, likes, and dislikes.

## Narrators

Narrators are the people who help the Storyteller present the adventure. They are impartial judges who describe scenes and events that cannot be staged, adjudicate rules, and occasionally play the roles of antagonists. Generally, enlisting the aid of one Narrator for every 10 players makes for a good ratio.

## Storyteller

Every game must have a Storyteller, who serves as the ultimate authority and final judge. The Storyteller creates the basic elements of the plot and makes sure that the story unfolds well. Storytelling is demanding but rewarding, as the Storyteller creates the framework upon which players build their experiences.

## Elegantly Simple

This game was designed to be easy to learn and easier to play. The Shining Host is a storytelling game. The rules are aimed at resolving conflicts quickly so that players can stay with the story without ever stepping outside their characters.

## The World of The Shining Host

The world of The Shining Host is, in many ways, just like our world. Children are born, grow up, go to school, follow careers, grow old and die. The sun rises and sets day after day. In the World of Darkness, however, everything is just a little grittier, a little more corrupt, and a little more tainted.

## Changelings

Changelings are fae souls born into human bodies. They remember the wonders of the Dreaming and struggle against Banality - the force of disbelief and mundanity that threatens to destroy all that is magical and wonderful. Changelings exist in two worlds: the mortal world and the fae world of the Dreaming.

## Key Themes

- **Magic and Wonder**: Changelings represent magic, wonder, and the power of dreams in a world of Banality.
- **The Struggle Against Banality**: Changelings must fight against disbelief and mundanity to preserve what is magical.
- **The Dreaming**: The realm of dreams and fae magic that changelings can access.
- **Dual Nature**: Changelings exist in both the mortal world and the fae world.
- **Loss and Hope**: Changelings have lost much but still fight to preserve wonder and magic.
- **The Hero's Journey**: Changelings often follow paths of adventure and discovery.

## Purpose and Value

MET The Shining Host serves as:
- The complete rulebook for playing Changeling: The Dreaming in live-action format
- A guide to the world of changelings and the Dreaming
- Information on character creation, Arts, Realms, and systems for MET Changeling
- Advice for both players and Storytellers on running successful games
- Exploration of themes of magic, wonder, and the struggle against Banality

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play or run live-action Changeling: The Dreaming games, providing all the tools needed to tell stories of fae souls struggling to preserve magic and wonder in a world of disbelief and mundanity.

# MET Mind's Eye Theatre Gift Deck

This is a deck of reference cards for Mind's Eye Theatre Werewolf: The Apocalypse, containing cards for all Garou Gifts. Each card provides the rules text for a specific Gift, organized by tribe, auspice, and breed, allowing players to quickly reference abilities during live-action play.

## Purpose and Scope

- Contains reference cards for all Garou Gifts from Laws of the Wild
- Each card lists a specific Gift with its rules text
- Organized by Gift type (Homid, Metis, Lupus, Theurge, etc.) and power level (Basic, Intermediate, Advanced)
- Includes tribe-specific Gifts (Black Furies, Bone Gnawers, Fianna, etc.)
- Designed for quick reference during live-action Werewolf games

## Structure

The deck includes Gifts organized by:

- **Breed Gifts**: Homid, Metis, Lupus
- **Auspice Gifts**: Ragabash, Theurge, Philodox, Galliard, Ahroun
- **Tribe Gifts**: Black Furies, Bone Gnawers, Children of Gaia, Fianna, Get of Fenris, Glass Walkers, Red Talons, Shadow Lords, Silent Striders, Silver Fangs, Stargazers, Uktena, Wendigo
- **Common Gifts**: Shared across multiple tribes or auspices

Each card provides:
- Gift name and type (Breed/Auspice/Tribe)
- Power level (Basic/Intermediate/Advanced)
- Complete rules text
- Gnosis costs and challenge requirements
- Special abilities and limitations

## Example Gifts

- **Homid Basic**: Jam Technology, Smell of Man
- **Metis Basic**: Curse of Hatred, Primal Anger
- **Theurge Basic**: Sense Wyrm, Create Element
- **Fianna Intermediate**: Reshape Object
- **Black Furies Basic**: Sense Wyrm
- And many more as detailed in Laws of the Wild

## Use

Players and Storytellers can use these cards to quickly reference Garou Gifts during play without interrupting the game flow to look up rules in Laws of the Wild.

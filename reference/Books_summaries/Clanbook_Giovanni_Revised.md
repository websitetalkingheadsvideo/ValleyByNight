# Clanbook: Giovanni (Revised)

This supplement for Vampire: The Masquerade provides comprehensive information on Clan Giovanni, the "Necromancers" known for their mastery of death magic and their incestuous family structure. The book explores the clan's history as a mortal family that became vampires, their necromantic practices, their complex family relationships, and their role as one of the most secretive and powerful independent clans.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book provides detailed information on Clan Giovanni for players and Storytellers.
- It explores the clan's history as a mortal family that became vampires.
- Examines the clan's necromantic practices and their relationship with the dead.
- Provides character creation options, new Disciplines, and information on Giovanni culture.

## Introduction: Kissing Cousins

The book opens with a story illustrating the complex and often disturbing family relationships within the Giovanni clan. A young woman attends a family gathering and finds herself caught in a web of family politics, blood bonds, and the clan's unique approach to maintaining their bloodline through carefully controlled relationships.

## Chapter One: A Familiar Concept

### The History of the Giovanni

The Giovanni trace their lineage to Augustus Giovanni, a mortal necromancer who discovered a way to achieve immortality. Unlike other clans, the Giovanni were not Embraced by an Antediluvian - they created their own path to vampirism through diablerie and necromantic rituals.

### The Family Business

The Giovanni are unique in that they are both a clan and a family. They maintain their mortal family structure even in undeath, with complex relationships, blood bonds, and carefully controlled breeding to maintain their bloodline. The clan is organized like a family business, with the eldest members holding the most power.

### Necromancy

The Giovanni's greatest asset is their mastery of necromancy. They can communicate with the dead, control wraiths, and manipulate the forces of death itself. This power sets them apart from other clans and makes them both feared and needed.

### The Proxy Kiss

The Giovanni use the "Proxy Kiss" - a process where mortals are given small amounts of vitae to prepare them for the Embrace. This creates ghouls who are bound to the clan and ready to become full Kindred when the time is right.

## Chapter Two: It Runs in the Family

### Clan Organization

The Giovanni are organized like a family business, with the eldest members holding the most power and authority. The clan maintains its mortal family structure, with complex relationships, blood bonds, and carefully controlled breeding.

### Family Ties

The Giovanni's family structure is both their greatest strength and their greatest weakness. Family loyalty is absolute, but family politics can be deadly. The clan's incestuous practices ensure that their bloodline remains pure, but they also create complex and often disturbing relationships.

### Necromantic Practices

The Giovanni's necromantic practices are central to their identity and power. They can communicate with the dead, control wraiths, and manipulate the forces of death. This power makes them valuable allies but also dangerous enemies.

### Relationships with Other Clans

The Giovanni have complex relationships with other clans:
- They are feared for their necromancy and their methods
- They are often needed for their ability to communicate with the dead
- They are distrusted because of their secretive nature and family practices
- They maintain their independence from both Camarilla and Sabbat

- **The Family**: The Giovanni's unique structure as both a clan and a family.
- **Necromancy**: The clan's mastery of death magic and communication with the dead.

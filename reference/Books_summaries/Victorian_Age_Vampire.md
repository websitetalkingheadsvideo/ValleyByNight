# Victorian Age - Vampire

This book presents a historical setting for Vampire: The Masquerade set during the Victorian era. The book provides information on playing vampires during this historical period, including the social, political, and cultural context of the era, and how Kindred society functioned during Victorian times.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book provides a historical setting for Vampire: The Masquerade.
- It focuses on the Victorian era as a time period for chronicles.
- Explores how Kindred society functioned during this historical period.
- Provides information on the social, political, and cultural context of the era.

## The Victorian Era

Information on the Victorian era as a historical setting:
- Social and cultural context
- Political landscape
- Technological developments
- How the era differs from modern times

## Kindred Society in the Victorian Age

Information on how vampires operated during this period:
- How the Masquerade was maintained
- Kindred politics and society
- The relationship between clans
- How the Traditions were interpreted

## Character Creation

Guidance on creating characters for Victorian-era chronicles:
- Historical character concepts
- Appropriate backgrounds for the era
- How to reflect the time period in characters
- Social class and its importance

## Setting Information

Detailed information on the Victorian setting:
- Major cities and locations
- Important historical events
- Cultural and social norms
- Technology and its limitations

## Key Themes

- **Historical Setting**: The book explores a specific historical period.
- **Social Class**: Victorian society was highly stratified, affecting both mortals and Kindred.
- **The Masquerade**: How vampires maintained secrecy in a different era.
- **Cultural Context**: The values and norms of Victorian society.
- **Historical Accuracy**: Incorporating real historical events and figures.

## Purpose and Value

Victorian Age - Vampire serves as:
- A historical setting for Vampire: The Masquerade chronicles
- Information on Kindred society during the Victorian era
- Guidance on creating characters appropriate to the time period
- Historical context and cultural information for the era

This book is useful for Storytellers and players who want to run or play in historical Vampire: The Masquerade chronicles set during the Victorian era, providing all the context and information needed to create an authentic Victorian setting for Kindred stories.

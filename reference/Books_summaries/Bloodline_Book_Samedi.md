# Bloodline Book: Samedi

This supplement for Vampire: The Masquerade provides comprehensive information on the Samedi bloodline, the "Zombies" known for their hideous, decaying appearance and their mastery of Thanatosis (the Discipline of death and decay). The book explores the bloodline's origins as an accident of Giovanni necromantic experimentation, their relationship with death and decay, and how they have turned their curse into a strength.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book provides detailed information on the Samedi bloodline for players and Storytellers.
- It explores the bloodline's origins and their relationship with the Giovanni.
- Examines the bloodline's unique appearance and their mastery of death magic.
- Provides character creation options, new Disciplines, and information on Samedi culture.

## Introduction

The book opens with a statement from the Samedi perspective, explaining that they are a reminder to all Kindred of their true nature - they are all dead, decaying, and returning to the soil. The Samedi see themselves as a reality check for other Kindred who try to ignore their true nature.

## Chapter One: The Real Scoop

### The Creation

The Samedi bloodline's creation has been blamed on both the Nosferatu and the Giovanni. However, the bloodline's true origin lies with the Giovanni. About 250 years ago, Antonio Giovanni stepped over the line in his necromantic experiments. In an attempt to capture a human soul at the moment of death and transfer it into the body of a vampire in torpor, something went very wrong.

### The Accident

Instead of transferring the soul into a living vampire, the Kindred was already experiencing Final Death. The unnaturally aged body had begun its rapid decomposition. The human victim had been suicidal, so it took precious moments to convince the Giovanni's creation that it should continue to exist.

### The First Samedi

By the time it was realized that the whole exercise was failing, the first Samedi had been created. Antonio tried to keep his mistake secret, but his necromantic curiosity got the better of him, and he couldn't bring himself to destroy this new species of Kindred. The first Samedi escaped, drained Antonio, and went on a rampage before finding shelter in a mausoleum.

### The Bloodline Spreads

This first Samedi, known as "Gran'daddy" or Paulo, retained the Giovanni's Necromancy discipline. The bloodline's obvious affinity with death and decay grew from this first creation, and the Samedi began to Embrace others, creating a bloodline that exists to remind all Kindred of their true nature.

## Key Themes

- **Death and Decay**: The Samedi's appearance and their mastery of death magic.
- **The Accident**: The bloodline's origin as a mistake in Giovanni necromantic experimentation.
- **Reality Check**: The Samedi's role as a reminder to all Kindred of their true nature.
- **Thanatosis**: The Samedi's unique Discipline of death and decay.
- **The Giovanni Connection**: The bloodline's complex relationship with the Giovanni who created them.

## Purpose and Value

Bloodline Book: Samedi serves as:
- Comprehensive information on the Samedi bloodline
- The history of the bloodline and their origin as a Giovanni accident
- Exploration of the bloodline's relationship with death and decay
- Character creation options for Samedi characters
- New Disciplines and powers, particularly Thanatosis
- Information on Samedi culture and their role as a reminder of Kindred's true nature

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play or understand Samedi characters, providing complete information on the "Zombies" and exploring how their hideous appearance and mastery of death magic have shaped them into a bloodline that serves as a stark reminder to all Kindred of their true nature as the dead.

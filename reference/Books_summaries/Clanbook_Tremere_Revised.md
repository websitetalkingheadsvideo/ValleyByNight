# Clanbook: Tremere (Revised)

This supplement for Vampire: The Masquerade provides comprehensive information on Clan Tremere, the "Warlocks" known for their blood magic (Thaumaturgy) and their rigid hierarchical organization. The book explores the clan's history as mortal wizards who became vampires, their pyramid of power, their magical practices, and their role as one of the most organized and powerful clans in Kindred society.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book provides detailed information on Clan Tremere for players and Storytellers.
- It explores the clan's history as wizards who became vampires.
- Examines the Tremere pyramid of power and organization.
- Provides character creation options, new Disciplines, and information on Tremere culture.

## Introduction: Enlightened by the Darkness

The book opens with a story of a mortal's Embrace into the Tremere clan, illustrating the clan's methods and the transformation from mortal magician to Kindred. The Embrace is presented as a ritualistic procedure, with the sire carefully observing the transformation and the awakening of magical potential. The story demonstrates the Tremere's scientific and methodical approach to everything, including the Embrace.

## Chapter One: The Price of Immortality

### The History of the Tremere

The Tremere are unique among the clans - they were not originally vampires. They were mortal wizards, members of the Order of Hermes, who discovered a way to achieve immortality through diablerie. They consumed the blood of a sleeping Antediluvian (Saulot, according to most accounts) and transformed themselves into vampires.

### The Transformation

The transformation from mortal wizards to vampires was not easy. The Tremere had to learn to adapt their magical practices to work with vitae instead of Quintessence. They developed Thaumaturgy, a form of blood magic that combines Hermetic principles with vampiric power.

### The Pyramid

The Tremere organized themselves into a rigid hierarchy known as the Pyramid. This structure ensures that all Tremere are answerable to their superiors, and information and power flow from the top down. The Pyramid is both the clan's greatest strength and its greatest weakness.

### The Gargoyles

The Tremere created the Gargoyles through magical experimentation, combining Tzimisce, Gangrel, and Nosferatu blood. For centuries, the Gargoyles served as the Tremere's guardians and enforcers. In recent nights, some Gargoyles have broken free from Tremere control, creating tension and conflict.

## Chapter Two: Inside the Pyramid

### Clan Organization

The Tremere have the most rigid and organized structure of any clan. Every Tremere has a place in the Pyramid, and advancement requires both ability and loyalty. The Pyramid ensures that the clan remains unified and that all Tremere work toward common goals.

### The Chantry

Tremere chantries are centers of learning, research, and power. They serve as havens, meeting places, and repositories of magical knowledge. Each chantry is part of the larger Pyramid structure, with a regent who answers to higher authorities.

### Thaumaturgy

Thaumaturgy is the Tremere's greatest asset. This form of blood magic allows them to perform feats that other Kindred cannot. The Tremere guard their magical secrets jealously, and learning Thaumaturgy requires both ability and loyalty to the clan.

### The Blood Bond

The Tremere use the blood bond extensively to ensure loyalty and control. Most Tremere are bound to their sires, and the chain of bonds extends up the Pyramid to the Inner Council. This system ensures loyalty but also creates resentment and rebellion.

### Relationships with Other Clans

The Tremere have complex relationships with other clans:
- They are feared for their magic and organization
- They are often distrusted because of their methods
- They have enemies among clans they have wronged (particularly the Tzimisce and Salubri)
- They are powerful allies but dangerous enemies

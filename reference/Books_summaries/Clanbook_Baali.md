# Clanbook: Baali

This supplement for Vampire: The Masquerade provides comprehensive information on the Baali, a bloodline (or possibly independent clan) known for their infernalism and their worship of demons. The book explores the Baali's history, their infernal practices, their various sects and beliefs, and their role as one of the most reviled and feared groups in Kindred society.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book provides detailed information on the Baali for players and Storytellers.
- It explores the bloodline's history and their infernal practices.
- Examines the various Baali sects and their different beliefs about demons.
- Provides character creation options, new Disciplines, and information on Baali culture.

## History

The Baali are a bloodline (or possibly an independent clan) that practices infernalism - the worship and service of demons. They are reviled by all other Kindred, and most Kindred will destroy Baali on sight. The Baali's exact origins are shrouded in mystery, but they are believed to be ancient and have been practicing infernalism for millennia.

### Infernalism

The Baali are defined by their practice of infernalism. They worship and serve demons, gaining power through infernal pacts and rituals. This practice sets them apart from all other Kindred and makes them enemies of everyone.

### The Various Sects

The Baali are divided into various sects, each with its own beliefs about demons and infernalism. Some believe they are serving the true gods, while others see demons as tools to be used. All are united by their practice of infernalism.

## Key Themes

- **Infernalism**: The Baali's practice of demon worship and infernal pacts.
- **Revulsion**: The universal hatred and fear that other Kindred feel toward the Baali.
- **Power Through Damnation**: The Baali's willingness to damn themselves for power.
- **Secrecy**: The Baali's need to hide their true nature from other Kindred.
- **The Various Sects**: The different Baali sects and their varying beliefs.

## Purpose and Value

Clanbook: Baali serves as:
- Comprehensive information on the Baali bloodline
- The history of the bloodline and their infernal practices
- Exploration of the various Baali sects and their beliefs
- Character creation options for Baali characters
- New Disciplines and powers related to infernalism
- Information on Baali culture and their role as reviled infernalists

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play or understand Baali characters, providing complete information on the infernalists and exploring how their practice of demon worship has shaped them into one of the most reviled and feared groups in Kindred society.

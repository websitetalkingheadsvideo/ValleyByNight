# Clanbook: Malkavian (Revised)

This supplement for Vampire: The Masquerade provides comprehensive information on Clan Malkavian, the "Lunatics" known for their madness, insight, and the Malkavian Madness Network. The book explores the clan's history, their unique perspective on reality, their role as seers and prophets, and the nature of their curse. The book examines how Malkavians navigate a world where their madness is both a burden and a gift.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book provides detailed information on Clan Malkavian for players and Storytellers.
- It explores the clan's madness and its various manifestations.
- Examines the Malkavian Madness Network and how Malkavians share information.
- Provides character creation options, new Disciplines, and information on Malkavian culture.

## Introduction: A Brief Procedure

The book opens with a story of a mortal's Embrace into the Malkavian clan, illustrating the clan's methods and the experience of joining the Malkavian Madness Network. The Embrace is presented as a scientific procedure, with the sire carefully observing the transformation from mortal to Kindred and the awakening of the Malkavian curse.

## Chapter One: The Tower of Abel

### The History of Madness

The Malkavians trace their lineage to Malkav, one of the Third Generation. The clan has always been associated with madness, prophecy, and insight. Unlike other clans, the Malkavians' curse is not a physical disfigurement but a mental one - they are all touched by madness in some form.

### Malkav and the Second City

Malkav was known as the Seer among the Antediluvians. He had the gift of prophecy and could see things that others could not. When the Second City fell, Malkav's gift became a curse, and his madness spread to all his descendants.

### The Malkavian Madness Network

All Malkavians are connected through the Malkavian Madness Network - a psychic link that allows them to share information, experiences, and insights. This network is both a blessing and a curse, as it means Malkavians can never truly be alone, but it also means they are constantly bombarded with the thoughts and experiences of other Malkavians.

### The Nature of Madness

Malkavian madness takes many forms. Some Malkavians are paranoid, others are delusional, some are obsessive-compulsive, and others have different mental conditions. What all Malkavians share is that their madness gives them insight into truths that others cannot see, but it also distorts their perception of reality.

### The Modern Malkavians

In the modern nights, the Malkavians have changed. The clan's madness has evolved, and new forms of insanity have emerged. Some Malkavians embrace their madness, while others fight against it. All must learn to live with it.

## Chapter Two: Inside Bedlam

### Clan Culture

The Malkavians have no formal organization or hierarchy. They are too chaotic and individualistic for such structures. Instead, they form loose networks based on shared delusions, common interests, or geographic proximity.

### The Madness Network

The Malkavian Madness Network is the clan's greatest asset and greatest burden. Through it, Malkavians can share information instantly, but they also share each other's pain, fear, and madness. The network is always active, and Malkavians can never truly escape it.

### Prophecy and Insight

Many Malkavians have the gift of prophecy, though their visions are often cryptic and difficult to interpret. They see patterns and connections that others miss, but their madness can also lead them to see patterns that don't exist.

### Relationships with Other Clans

The Malkavians have complex relationships with other clans:
- They are often dismissed as harmless lunatics
- Some clans fear their insight and prophecy
- They are sometimes used as tools or pawns by more powerful Kindred
- Their madness makes them unpredictable allies or enemies

## Key Themes

- **Madness**: The clan's curse and how it manifests in different Malkavians.
- **Insight**: The gift of seeing truths that others cannot, even if those truths are distorted.
- **The Network**: The Malkavian Madness Network and how it connects all Malkavians.
- **Prophecy**: The clan's role as seers and prophets in Kindred society.
- **Reality**: The question of what is real and what is delusion for Malkavians.
- **Isolation and Connection**: Malkavians are both isolated by their madness and connected through the network.

# MET Mind's Eye Theatre Discipline Deck

This is a deck of reference cards for Mind's Eye Theatre Vampire: The Masquerade, containing cards for all Disciplines and their powers. Each card provides the rules text for a specific Discipline power, allowing players to quickly reference abilities during live-action play without needing to consult the rulebook.

## Purpose and Scope

- Contains reference cards for all Disciplines from Laws of the Night
- Each card lists a specific Discipline power with its rules text
- Organized by Discipline (Animalism, Auspex, Celerity, etc.) and power level (Basic, Intermediate, Advanced)
- Includes page references to Laws of the Night for full details
- Designed for quick reference during live-action play

## Structure

The deck is organized by Discipline, with cards for:

- **Animalism**: Feral Whispers, Beckoning, Quell the Beast, Subsume the Spirit, Drawing Out the Beast
- **Auspex**: Heightened Senses, Aura Perception, The Spirit's Touch, Telepathy, Psychic Projection
- **Celerity**: Various speed-enhancing powers
- **Dominate**: Command and control powers
- **Fortitude**: Damage resistance powers
- **Obfuscate**: Stealth and concealment powers
- **Potence**: Physical strength powers
- **Presence**: Social manipulation powers
- **Protean**: Shape-changing powers
- **Thaumaturgy**: Blood magic paths and rituals
- And other Disciplines as detailed in Laws of the Night

Each card provides:
- Discipline name and power level
- Power name
- Page reference to Laws of the Night
- Complete rules text for the power
- Special abilities, costs, and limitations

## Use

Players and Storytellers can use these cards to quickly reference Discipline powers during play without interrupting the game flow to look up rules in the main rulebook.

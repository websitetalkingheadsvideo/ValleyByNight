# MET Mind's Eye Theatre Prop Deck

This is a deck of prop cards for Mind's Eye Theatre games, containing reference cards for various game mechanics and traits used across different game lines. The deck includes cards for Pathos Traits (Wraith), Rage Traits (Werewolf), Gnosis Traits (Werewolf), Glamour Traits (Changeling), and Blood Traits (Vampire).

## Purpose and Scope

- Contains prop cards for various supernatural traits used in MET games
- Each card type provides rules text for how that trait functions
- Includes cards for multiple game lines: Vampire, Werewolf, Wraith, Changeling
- Designed for quick reference and physical representation during live-action play

## Card Types

### Pathos Trait (Wraith: The Oblivion)

**Special Abilities:** One Pathos Trait is sufficient to:
- Heal one Corpus Level of normal damage
- Power certain Arcanoi, as listed in Oblivion

**Description:** As shadows of the once-living, wraiths can only hold on to the material world through the memory of their strongest passions.

### Rage Trait (Werewolf: The Apocalypse)

**Special Abilities:** One Rage Trait is sufficient to:
- Change into another form immediately
- Take an extra action during a challenge
- Make a follow-up attack on a successful attack
- Heal one Health Level of damage after becoming Incapacitated
- Power certain Gifts as listed in Laws of the Wild

**Description:** The rage of werewolves is mighty, turning them into creatures of incredible destructive power in combat.

### Gnosis Trait (Werewolf: The Apocalypse)

**Special Abilities:** One Gnosis Trait is sufficient to:
- Step into the Umbra
- Power certain Garou Gifts, as listed in Laws of the Wild

**Description:** Werewolves call upon these energies to invoke powers of the spirit.

### Glamour Trait (Changeling: The Dreaming)

**Special Abilities:** One Glamour Trait is sufficient to:
- Enchant a mortal for one day
- Add a single Bunk to a cantrip
- Heal one Health Level of normal chimerical damage
- Power certain cantrips or Birthrights, as listed in The Shining Host

**Description:** Ephemeral and intangible, Glamour is the energy of pure dreamstuff, used by faeries.

### Blood Trait (Vampire: The Masquerade)

**Special Abilities:** One Blood Trait is sufficient to:
- Heal one Health Level of normal damage
- Gain one temporary Physical Trait
- Turn a mortal into a ghoul for one month
- Sustain a vampire for one night

**Description:** The blood of mortals (and other vampires) sustains the undead, providing both nourishment and power.

## Use

These prop cards can be used physically during live-action games to represent traits, making it easier for players and Storytellers to track resources and abilities without constant reference to rulebooks.

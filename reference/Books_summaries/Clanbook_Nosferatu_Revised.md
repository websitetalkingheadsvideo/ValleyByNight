# Clanbook: Nosferatu (Revised)

This supplement for Vampire: The Masquerade provides comprehensive information on Clan Nosferatu, the "Sewer Rats" known for their hideous appearance and their role as informants and spies. The book explores the clan's history, their underground kingdoms, their network of information, and how they have turned their curse into a strength. The book examines how Nosferatu navigate a world that shuns them for their appearance but needs them for their knowledge.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book provides detailed information on Clan Nosferatu for players and Storytellers.
- It explores the clan's curse and how they have adapted to it.
- Examines the clan's underground kingdoms and information networks.
- Provides character creation options, new Disciplines, and information on Nosferatu culture.

## Introduction: Food Chain

The book opens with a story of a mortal's Embrace into the Nosferatu clan, illustrating the horror of the transformation and the loss of beauty. A woman who was obsessed with her appearance finds herself transformed into a hideous creature, forced to abandon her old identity and become someone else. The story demonstrates the psychological impact of the Nosferatu curse.

## Chapter One: Legends and History

### The Origins

The Nosferatu trace their lineage to Absimiliard, one of the Third Generation. According to legend, Absimiliard was cursed by Caine for his vanity, and this curse has been passed down to all his descendants. Every Nosferatu is hideously deformed, making it impossible for them to blend in with mortals.

### The Curse

The Nosferatu curse is unique among the clans - it is a physical disfigurement that makes them instantly recognizable as monsters. No Nosferatu can pass for human, and they must hide from mortals or risk breaking the Masquerade. This curse has shaped every aspect of Nosferatu culture and society.

### The Underground Kingdoms

Because they cannot move freely among mortals, the Nosferatu have created vast underground kingdoms in the sewers, subways, and forgotten places beneath cities. These warrens serve as havens, meeting places, and repositories of information.

### The Information Network

The Nosferatu have turned their curse into a strength by becoming the information brokers of Kindred society. They know everything that happens in a city because they are everywhere - in the sewers, the subways, the forgotten places. Other Kindred need their information, which gives the Nosferatu power despite their appearance.

## Chapter Two: Inside Clan Nosferatu

### Clan Culture

The Nosferatu have developed a unique culture based on their shared curse. They value information, survival, and loyalty to each other. They have learned to find beauty in things other than appearance, and they have developed a dark sense of humor about their situation.

### The Warrens

Nosferatu warrens are complex underground networks that serve multiple purposes. They are havens, meeting places, information repositories, and sometimes entire communities. The warrens are the heart of Nosferatu society.

### Information and Secrets

Information is the Nosferatu's greatest asset. They collect secrets, rumors, and knowledge from every corner of the city. This information gives them power and influence far beyond what their appearance would suggest.

### Relationships with Other Clans

The Nosferatu have complex relationships with other clans:
- They are often dismissed or shunned for their appearance
- Other clans need their information but are uncomfortable around them
- They have a reputation for being untrustworthy, though this is often undeserved
- They are sometimes used as tools or pawns by more powerful Kindred

## Key Themes

- **The Curse**: The physical disfigurement that marks all Nosferatu and shapes their existence.
- **Beauty and Ugliness**: The loss of beauty and the search for meaning beyond appearance.
- **The Underground**: The Nosferatu's world beneath the city, in the sewers and forgotten places.
- **Information**: The clan's greatest asset - knowledge and secrets that give them power.
- **Isolation**: The Nosferatu's separation from both mortals and other Kindred.
- **Adaptation**: How the Nosferatu have turned their curse into a strength.

# MET Laws of the Resurrection

This book is the core rulebook for Mind's Eye Theatre Mummy: The Resurrection, providing all the material that players and Storytellers need for creating, playing, and Storytelling mortals who have joined with ancient Egyptian souls and received the gift of immortality. The book presents the Reborn - humans who have been resurrected through a mystical process involving ancient Egyptian spirits called ba, returning to life with purpose and power.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book contains all the information needed to play Mind's Eye Theatre Mummy: The Resurrection.
- It is based on the tabletop game Mummy: The Resurrection, adapted for live-action play.
- Focuses on mortals who have been resurrected through joining with ancient Egyptian souls.
- Provides rules for character creation, Hekau (mystical powers), and Storytelling.

## Chapter One: Introduction and Setting

### What is Storytelling?

The book emphasizes storytelling as one of the oldest forms of human communication. Live-action roleplaying (LARP) is presented as a return to the oldest form of participatory storytelling, where storytellers relied on their audience for reactions.

### What is Mind's Eye Theatre?

Playing Mind's Eye Theatre is like being in a movie. You and your friends are the characters, but there is no script. The Storyteller creates the framework, then turns players loose to see what happens. Most scenes are played out in real time, and always in character.

### Characters

When you play Laws of the Resurrection, you take on the persona of a Reborn - a human who has joined with an ancient Egyptian soul and received the gift of immortality. Your character can be almost anything - from any walk of life, age, creed, race or sex. The only limit is your imagination.

Creating a character is easy and takes just a few minutes. However, a character should be more than just numbers - characters have pasts, likes and dislikes, goals and dreams, all the intangible things that make a person complete.

### The Storyteller

The Storyteller is the one who creates the world that the players move through. She creates a skeletal framework of setting and plot, then turns the characters loose to put flesh on its bones. The Storyteller is usually assisted by Narrators, who play their own characters but are ready to answer rules questions when necessary.

### Elegantly Simple

This game was designed to be easy to play and easier to start. Character creation takes only a few minutes. The basic rules are simple, and they cover most of the encounters that a new player will enter.

## Chapter Two: The Amenti

This chapter describes the various mummies based on the piece of soul that joins with their own. The Amenti are the different types of Reborn, each associated with different aspects of ancient Egyptian mythology and different purposes in their resurrected existence.

## Chapter Three: Character Creation

This chapter covers the process of creating Reborn characters, including:
- Attributes and Abilities
- Backgrounds
- The process of joining with a ba (ancient Egyptian soul)
- Character concepts and backgrounds
- The journey through Duat (the Underworld) that precedes resurrection

## Chapter Four: Hekau

Detailed information on Hekau - the mystical powers of ancient Egypt that the Reborn wield against their enemies. Hekau are magical abilities based on ancient Egyptian concepts, allowing the Reborn to perform supernatural feats. The chapter covers:
- How Hekau work
- Different types of Hekau
- Using Hekau in play
- The connection to ancient Egyptian magic and mythology

## Chapter Five: Rules, Systems and Drama

The core rules system for resolving actions, conflicts, and challenges. The rules are designed to be elegantly simple, allowing quick resolution while maintaining story integrity. Covers:
- Basic conflict resolution
- Combat systems
- Social interactions
- Using Hekau
- Drama systems

## Chapter Six: Storytelling

Guidance for Storytellers on how to run successful Mummy: The Resurrection games, including:
- Creating compelling stories
- Managing the mystery of the ba and resurrection
- Working with ancient Egyptian themes
- Creating memorable antagonists
- Balancing the Reborn's purpose with their humanity

## Chapter Seven: Allies and Antagonists

Information on the various beings and organizations that the Reborn might encounter, including:
- Other supernatural creatures
- Mortal organizations
- Ancient Egyptian entities
- Threats and enemies

## Key Themes

- **Resurrection and Purpose**: The Reborn have been given a second chance at life with a specific purpose.
- **Ancient Egyptian Mythology**: The connection to ancient Egypt, its gods, and its magical traditions.
- **The Journey Through Duat**: The mystical journey through the Underworld that precedes resurrection.
- **The Ba**: The ancient Egyptian soul that joins with the mortal, granting immortality and purpose.
- **Balance Between Life and Death**: The Reborn exist between the worlds of the living and the dead.
- **The Mission**: Each Reborn has a purpose they must fulfill in their resurrected existence.

## Purpose and Value

MET Laws of the Resurrection serves as:
- The complete rulebook for playing Mummy: The Resurrection in live-action format
- A guide to the world of the Reborn and their connection to ancient Egypt
- Information on character creation, Hekau, and systems for MET Mummy
- Advice for both players and Storytellers on running successful games

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play or run live-action Mummy: The Resurrection games, providing all the tools needed to tell stories of mortals who have been resurrected with purpose and power, connected to the ancient mysteries of Egypt.

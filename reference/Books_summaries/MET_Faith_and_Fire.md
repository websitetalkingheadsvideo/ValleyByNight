# MET Mind's Eye Theatre Faith and Fire

This supplement for Mind's Eye Theatre presents information on faith, religion, and the power of True Faith in the World of Darkness. The book explores how mortals with strong faith can affect supernatural creatures, the role of religion in the World of Darkness, and how to incorporate faith-based elements into games.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book is a supplement providing information on faith and religion in the World of Darkness.
- It focuses on True Faith and its effects on supernatural creatures.
- Explores various religions and their role in the World of Darkness.
- Provides Storyteller resources for incorporating faith-based elements into games.

## True Faith

Information on True Faith - the power that mortals with strong faith can wield:
- What True Faith is
- How True Faith works
- The effects of True Faith on supernatural creatures
- How to incorporate True Faith into games

## Religion in the World of Darkness

Information on various religions and their role:
- Different faiths and their practices
- How religions interact with the supernatural
- Religious organizations and their goals
- The role of faith in mortal and supernatural society

## Faith-Based Characters

Information on creating and playing characters with faith:
- Character concepts involving faith
- How faith affects character abilities
- Roleplaying faithful characters
- The relationship between faith and the supernatural

## Key Themes

- **The Power of Faith**: True Faith can affect even the most powerful supernatural creatures.
- **Religion and the Supernatural**: How various religions interact with the World of Darkness.
- **Moral Choices**: Characters must navigate the relationship between faith and the supernatural.
- **Protection and Power**: Faith can both protect mortals and give them power over monsters.

## Purpose and Value

MET Faith and Fire serves as:
- A supplement providing information on faith and religion in the World of Darkness
- Information on True Faith and its effects
- Storyteller resources for incorporating faith-based elements into games
- Material for exploring the relationship between religion and the supernatural

This book is useful for Storytellers and players who want to incorporate faith and religion into their games, providing information on True Faith, various religions, and how to use these elements in World of Darkness chronicles.

# Clanbook: Lasombra

This supplement for Vampire: The Masquerade provides comprehensive information on Clan Lasombra, the "Keepers" known for their mastery of Obtenebration (the Discipline of shadow manipulation) and their role as one of the founding clans of the Sabbat. The book explores the clan's history, their relationship with shadows and darkness, their role in Sabbat leadership, and their aristocratic nature despite their rejection of the Camarilla.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book provides detailed information on Clan Lasombra for players and Storytellers.
- It explores the clan's history and their role in the Sabbat.
- Examines the clan's mastery of Obtenebration and their relationship with shadows.
- Provides character creation options, new Disciplines, and information on Lasombra culture.

## History

The Lasombra trace their lineage to Lasombra, one of the Third Generation. The clan has always been associated with shadows, darkness, and power. They were one of the founding clans of the Sabbat, and they continue to serve as leaders and enforcers within the sect.

### The Sabbat

The Lasombra are one of the two founding clans of the Sabbat (along with the Tzimisce). They provide much of the sect's leadership and organization. The clan's aristocratic nature and focus on power make them natural leaders, even within a sect that rejects the Camarilla's traditions.

### Obtenebration

Obtenebration is the Lasombra's unique Discipline, allowing them to manipulate shadows and darkness. This power reflects the clan's nature as masters of darkness and their ability to control what others cannot see.

### The Clan's Nature

The Lasombra are aristocratic and power-hungry, even within the Sabbat. They value strength, competence, and the ability to lead. They are known for their ruthlessness and their willingness to do whatever is necessary to achieve their goals.

## Key Themes

- **Shadows and Darkness**: The Lasombra's mastery of Obtenebration and their relationship with darkness.
- **Sabbat Leadership**: The clan's role as leaders and enforcers within the Sabbat.
- **Power**: The Lasombra's focus on power, strength, and competence.
- **Aristocracy**: The clan's aristocratic nature despite their rejection of the Camarilla.
- **Ruthlessness**: The Lasombra's willingness to do whatever is necessary to achieve their goals.

## Purpose and Value

Clanbook: Lasombra serves as:
- Comprehensive information on Clan Lasombra
- The history of the clan and their role in the Sabbat
- Exploration of the clan's mastery of Obtenebration
- Character creation options for Lasombra characters
- New Disciplines and powers
- Information on Lasombra culture and their role in Sabbat leadership

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play or understand Lasombra characters, providing complete information on the "Keepers" and exploring how their mastery of shadows and their role in the Sabbat have shaped them into one of the most powerful and feared clans in Kindred society.

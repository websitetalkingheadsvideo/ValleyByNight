# 🔥 *Laws of Judgment* — Core Summary  
*A Mind’s Eye Theatre guide to running the End Times across all World of Darkness venues.*

## 🌑 Purpose of the Book  
- Serves as the **final Mind’s Eye Theatre (MET)** volume, designed to bring LARP chronicles to their ultimate conclusion.  
- Provides **End‑Times scenarios** for every major supernatural line: Vampire, Werewolf, Mage, Changeling, Hunter, Mummy, Wraith, and Kuei‑jin.  
- Offers **Storyteller‑focused tools** for structuring climactic plots, managing chaos, and delivering emotionally powerful finales.

---

# 📘 Structure & Chapter Themes

## **Introduction – “The Signs Are on Fire”**  
- Frames the book as a culmination of MET’s long history.  
- Emphasizes that endings give meaning to stories.  
- Encourages Storytellers to adapt material freely to suit their chronicle’s tone and needs.

# 🧍‍♂️ **Chapter One: Judgment Day (Mortals & Hunters)**  
Explores how **ordinary humans** and mortal organizations experience the end of the world.  
Key elements:  
- Rising global chaos: disasters, disease, political collapse, mass panic.  
- Spotlight on mortal factions:  
  - **Inquisition / Society of Leopold**  
  - **Arcanum**  
  - **Twilight Agencies** (SAD, NSA, etc.)  
  - **Dauntain**  
  - **Orphic Circle**  
  - **Benandanti**  
- Each group receives multiple scenario paths showing how they might react, collapse, or influence events.

---

# 🩸 **Chapter Two: Gehenna (Vampires)**  
A toolkit for running the Final Nights for **Laws of the Night**.  
Major mechanics and themes:  
- **The Withering**: Generationally cascading decay of vampiric power.  
- **Clan Curses Intensify**: Each clan’s weakness becomes extreme or grotesque.  
- **Severed Bloodlines**: When an Antediluvian dies, its clan suffers madness, frenzy, and violent diablerie.  
- **Diablerie as Temptation**: A desperate way to temporarily stave off the Withering.  
- **Telltale Signs**: Earthquakes, plagues, riots, and madness as apocalyptic omens.

---

# 🌍 What the Book Provides Overall  
- **Multiple End‑Times scenarios** for each supernatural type.  
- **Mechanics** to represent cosmic collapse, spiritual decay, and supernatural upheaval.  
- **Storyteller guidance** on pacing, mood, symbolism, and player experience.  
- A strong emphasis on **customization**: the chronicle belongs to the Storyteller, not the book.

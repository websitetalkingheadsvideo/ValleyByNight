# Clanbook: Daughters of Cacophony

This supplement for Vampire: The Masquerade provides comprehensive information on the Daughters of Cacophony, a bloodline known for their mastery of Melpominee (the Discipline of voice and song). The book explores the bloodline's history, their relationship with music and voice, their all-female membership, and their role as performers and artists in Kindred society.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book provides detailed information on the Daughters of Cacophony bloodline for players and Storytellers.
- It explores the bloodline's history and their relationship with music.
- Examines the bloodline's all-female membership and their Discipline of Melpominee.
- Provides character creation options, new Disciplines, and information on Daughters of Cacophony culture.

## History

The Daughters of Cacophony are a bloodline, possibly descended from the Toreador or another clan. They are known for being an all-female bloodline (though some sources suggest there may be male members) and for their mastery of Melpominee, the Discipline of voice and song.

### Music and Voice

The Daughters of Cacophony are defined by their relationship with music and voice. They are performers, singers, and artists who use their voices as both art and weapon. Their Discipline of Melpominee allows them to create powerful effects through song and voice.

### The All-Female Bloodline

The Daughters of Cacophony are known as an all-female bloodline, though the exact nature of this restriction varies in different sources. They Embrace only women, creating a bloodline that is entirely female.

### Performance and Art

The Daughters of Cacophony are performers and artists. They use their voices and music as both art and power. They are often found in the world of entertainment, using their abilities to influence and control through performance.

## Key Themes

- **Music and Voice**: The Daughters' mastery of Melpominee and their relationship with music.
- **All-Female**: The bloodline's restriction to female members.
- **Performance**: The Daughters' role as performers and artists.
- **Art as Power**: The bloodline's use of music and voice as both art and weapon.

## Purpose and Value

Clanbook: Daughters of Cacophony serves as:
- Comprehensive information on the Daughters of Cacophony bloodline
- The history of the bloodline and their relationship with music
- Exploration of the bloodline's all-female nature and their Discipline of Melpominee
- Character creation options for Daughters of Cacophony characters
- New Disciplines and powers, particularly Melpominee
- Information on Daughters of Cacophony culture and their role as performers

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play or understand Daughters of Cacophony characters, providing complete information on the all-female bloodline of performers and exploring how their mastery of voice and song has shaped them into a unique bloodline in Kindred society.

# Laws of the East

The document is the core rulebook for Mind's Eye Theatre Kindred of the East (Kuei-jin), providing all essential rules, systems, and information needed to play and run live-action roleplaying games featuring the Hungry Dead of the Middle Kingdom. The book presents a complete system for playing Asian vampires who have returned from the Underworld to fulfill unfinished destinies.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book provides all the information needed to play and run Mind's Eye Theatre games featuring Kuei-jin (Kindred of the East).
- It is based on the tabletop game Kindred of the East, adapted for live-action play.
- It presents the unique cosmology, culture, and mechanics of the Hungry Dead.
- The book assumes familiarity with basic Mind's Eye Theatre concepts but provides complete rules for Kuei-jin characters.

## Introduction

The book introduces players to the world of the Kuei-jin, vampires who have returned from the hellish ravages of the Underworld. Unlike Western vampires, Kuei-jin are souls that have reanimated their bodies after death, creating a unique form of undeath. Players take on personas of these Hungry Dead who must balance their higher selves (Hun) with their demonic nature (P'o) while following one of the Five Dharmas.

### The Middle Kingdom

The setting focuses on Asia and areas with significant Asian populations, where the Kuei-jin hold sway. The cosmology is based on Chinese philosophy, with concepts of Yin and Yang, Chi (spiritual energy), and the Wheel of Ages.

### The Hungry Dead

Kuei-jin are fundamentally different from Western vampires. They are souls that have returned from the Underworld, reanimating their bodies. They must balance their Hun (higher soul) and P'o (demon soul) while following a Dharma (path of enlightenment).

## Chapter One: Introduction and Setting

Provides an overview of the Kuei-jin, their origins, and the Middle Kingdom setting. Explains the cosmology of Yin and Yang Worlds, the Yomi World (realm of demons), and the current Fifth Age (Age of Darkness).

### The History of the Wan Kuei

The book details the fall of the Wan Xian (the Ten Thousand Immortals), who were cursed by the August Personage of Jade for their greed and disobedience. This curse created the Wan Kuei (Ten Thousand Demons), who must now feed on Chi to survive.

### The Wheel of Ages

The cosmology includes the concept of Ages that cycle through time. The current Fifth Age is the Age of Darkness, with the feared Sixth Age approaching when the Yama Kings may emerge triumphant.

## Chapter Two: The Amenti

Detailed information on the various types of Kuei-jin (Amenti) and their characteristics:

- **The Five Courts**: Different regional organizations of Kuei-jin
- **Dharma Paths**: The Five Dharmas that guide Kuei-jin existence
- **The Hun and P'o**: The dual nature of every Kuei-jin soul
- **Chi**: The spiritual energy that sustains the Hungry Dead

## Chapter Three: Character Creation

Complete character creation system for Kuei-jin:

- **Attributes**: Physical, Social, and Mental traits
- **Abilities**: Talents, Skills, and Knowledges
- **Disciplines**: Supernatural powers unique to Kuei-jin
- **Dharma**: Choosing one of the Five Dharmas
- **Chi**: Managing spiritual energy
- **Backgrounds**: Resources and advantages available to characters

The system includes unique elements like the re (training period), bu (running monkey period), and the selection of a Dharma path.

### The Five Dharmas

- **The Howl of the Devil-Tiger (P'o)**: Embracing the demon within
- **The Way of the Resplendent Crane (Hun)**: Following the higher soul
- **The Song of the Shadow (Yin)**: Balancing through darkness
- **The Path of a Thousand Whispers (Balance)**: Maintaining equilibrium
- **The Dance of the Thrashing Dragon (Yang)**: Embracing action and passion

## Chapter Four: Hekau

The mystical powers of ancient Egypt, adapted for Kuei-jin. Hekau are the supernatural abilities available to the Hungry Dead:

- **Basic Hekau**: Entry-level powers
- **Intermediate Hekau**: More powerful abilities
- **Advanced Hekau**: Master-level powers
- **Unique Disciplines**: Powers specific to certain Dharma paths or courts

## Chapter Five: Rules, Systems and Drama

Comprehensive rules for gameplay:

- **Basic Rules**: Core mechanics for challenges and conflicts
- **Chi Systems**: Rules for managing spiritual energy
- **Combat Systems**: Rules for physical confrontations
- **Dharma Challenges**: Tests of a character's adherence to their path
- **The P'o**: Rules for when the demon soul takes control
- **Yin and Yang Balance**: Maintaining spiritual equilibrium
- **The Second Breath**: The process of becoming Kuei-jin

## Chapter Six: Storytelling

Guidance for Storytellers and Narrators running Kuei-jin chronicles:

- **Creating Stories**: How to craft plots appropriate for the Middle Kingdom
- **Running Games**: Practical advice for managing players and scenes
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Guidelines for respectfully portraying Asian cultures
- **Dharma and Morality**: How to handle the unique morality system
- **Incorporating Eastern Philosophy**: Using concepts of karma, balance, and destiny

## Chapter Seven: Allies and Antagonists

Information on other denizens of the Middle Kingdom:

- **Other Shen**: Werewolves (Hengeyokai), Mages, and other Asian supernaturals
- **Yama Kings**: Powerful demons from the Yomi World
- **Mortals**: Hunters, police, and other human threats
- **Western Vampires**: Interactions with Kindred from other regions
- **Spirits**: Various entities from the Yin and Yang Worlds

## Key Themes

- **Balance**: The constant struggle between Hun and P'o, Yin and Yang
- **Dharma**: Following a path of enlightenment while being a predator
- **Karma**: The consequences of actions in this life and beyond
- **The Fifth Age**: Living in an age of darkness and approaching doom
- **Cultural Identity**: Maintaining Asian traditions in a modern world
- **The Second Breath**: The unique nature of Kuei-jin existence

## Purpose and Value

Laws of the East serves as:

- The complete core rulebook for Mind's Eye Theatre Kindred of the East
- A comprehensive guide to Kuei-jin character creation and gameplay
- Reference material for all Dharmas, Hekau, and game systems
- Storytelling advice for running chronicles in the Middle Kingdom
- Essential foundation for all MET Kindred of the East play

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play or run Mind's Eye Theatre games featuring Kuei-jin, providing all the rules, systems, and cultural information needed to create and tell stories in the Middle Kingdom setting.

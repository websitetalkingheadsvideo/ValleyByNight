# Clanbook: Cappadocian

This supplement for Vampire: The Masquerade provides comprehensive information on the Cappadocians, an ancient clan that was largely destroyed by the Giovanni. The book explores the clan's history, their focus on death and the study of mortality, their relationship with the Giovanni, and the few surviving Cappadocians who remain in the modern nights.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book provides detailed information on the Cappadocian clan for players and Storytellers.
- It explores the clan's history and their focus on the study of death.
- Examines the clan's destruction at the hands of the Giovanni.
- Provides character creation options, new Disciplines, and information on Cappadocian culture.

## History

The Cappadocians trace their lineage to Cappadocius, one of the Third Generation. The clan was known for their scholarly nature and their focus on the study of death and mortality. They were one of the most ancient and respected clans until their destruction.

### The Study of Death

The Cappadocians were scholars who dedicated themselves to understanding death, mortality, and what lies beyond. They studied necromancy, the nature of the soul, and the mysteries of Final Death. This scholarly focus set them apart from other clans.

### The Giovanni Betrayal

The Cappadocians were largely destroyed by the Giovanni, who were originally a mortal family that served the Cappadocians. The Giovanni learned necromancy from their masters, then turned on them, diablerizing many Cappadocian elders and effectively destroying the clan.

### The Survivors

A few Cappadocians survived the Giovanni betrayal. These survivors are ancient, powerful, and focused on revenge against the Giovanni. They maintain the old ways and continue the Cappadocian tradition of studying death.

## Key Themes

- **The Study of Death**: The Cappadocians' scholarly focus on death and mortality.
- **The Betrayal**: The Giovanni's destruction of the clan.
- **The Survivors**: The few remaining Cappadocians and their quest for revenge.
- **Necromancy**: The Cappadocians' mastery of death magic.
- **Scholarship**: The clan's focus on knowledge and understanding.

## Purpose and Value

Clanbook: Cappadocian serves as:
- Comprehensive information on the Cappadocian clan
- The history of the clan and their focus on the study of death
- Exploration of the Giovanni betrayal and the clan's destruction
- Character creation options for Cappadocian characters
- New Disciplines and powers
- Information on Cappadocian culture and the few survivors

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play or understand Cappadocian characters, providing complete information on the ancient scholars of death and exploring how their focus on understanding mortality led to their destruction at the hands of their former servants, the Giovanni.

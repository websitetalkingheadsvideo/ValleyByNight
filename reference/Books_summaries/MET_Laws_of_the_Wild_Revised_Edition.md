# MET Laws of the Wild Revised Edition

This book is the revised and updated rulebook for playing the mighty Garou (werewolves) in live-action format for Mind's Eye Theatre. Based on Werewolf: The Apocalypse Revised, this book carries the new material and the advanced storyline. Everything a player needs to learn the basics of the Werewolf setting, create a character, and begin playing is contained within.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book is the revised edition of Laws of the Wild, updated to match Werewolf: The Apocalypse Revised.
- It contains all the information needed to start playing Mind's Eye Theatre Werewolf: The Apocalypse.
- It is based on the tabletop game Werewolf: The Apocalypse Revised, adapted for live-action play.
- Provides elegantly simple rules designed to be easy to play and easier to start.

## Chapter One: Introduction

### Stories Around the Fire

The book emphasizes storytelling as one of the oldest forms of human communication. Live-action roleplaying (LARP) is presented as a return to the oldest form of participatory storytelling.

### What is Mind's Eye Theatre?

Playing Mind's Eye Theatre is like being in a movie. You and your friends are the characters, but there is no script. The Storyteller creates the framework, then turns players loose to see what happens. Most scenes are played out in real-time, and always in character.

### Characters

When you play Apocalypse, you take on another persona - here a werewolf. Your character can be almost anything - from any walk of life, age, creed, race or sex. The only limit is your imagination.

Creating a character is easy and takes just a few minutes. However, a character should be more than just numbers - characters have pasts, likes and dislikes, goals and dreams, all the intangible things that make a person complete.

### The Storyteller

The Storyteller is the one who creates the world that the players move through. She creates a skeletal framework of setting and plot, then turns the characters loose to put flesh on its bones. The Storyteller is usually assisted by Narrators, who play their own characters but are ready to answer rules questions when necessary.

### Werewolf: The Apocalypse and Laws of the Wild

This book is based on the tabletop creation Werewolf: The Apocalypse Revised. While it is not necessary to own or know Werewolf to play this game, some players may find it helpful for more setting material. This edition has been created to update the rules for Werewolf's live-action adaptation, Apocalypse.

### Elegantly Simple

This game was designed to be easy to play and easier to start. Character creation takes only a few minutes. The basic rules are simple, and they cover most of the encounters a new player will enter.

## Key Themes

- **The War Against the Wyrm**: The Garou's eternal struggle to defend Gaia from corruption and destruction.
- **The Imminent Apocalypse**: The sense that these are the last days and the final battle is approaching.
- **Tribal Unity and Division**: The need for tribes to work together despite their differences.
- **The Umbra**: The spiritual realm that the Garou can access and its importance to their culture.
- **Rage and Frenzy**: The dangerous power that drives the Garou but can also consume them.
- **The Veil**: The necessity of hiding the Garou's existence from humanity.

## Purpose and Value

MET Laws of the Wild Revised Edition serves as:
- The complete revised rulebook for playing Werewolf: The Apocalypse in live-action format
- An updated version matching Werewolf: The Apocalypse Revised
- A guide to the Garou's world, culture, and war against the Wyrm
- Information on character creation, Gifts, Rites, and systems for MET Werewolf
- Advice for both players and Storytellers on running successful games

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play or run live-action Werewolf: The Apocalypse games using the revised rules and storyline, providing all the tools needed to tell stories of the Garou's struggle against the Wyrm in the final nights before the Apocalypse.

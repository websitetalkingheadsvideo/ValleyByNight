# MET Oblivion

This book is the core rulebook for Mind's Eye Theatre Wraith: The Oblivion, providing all the material that players and Storytellers need for creating, playing, and Storytelling wraiths - the restless dead who exist in the Shadowlands, caught between life and true death. The book presents the world of the dead, where wraiths struggle with their Passions, their Shadows, and the ever-present threat of Oblivion.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book contains all the information needed to play Mind's Eye Theatre Wraith: The Oblivion.
- It is based on the tabletop game Wraith: The Oblivion, adapted for live-action play.
- Focuses on wraiths - the restless dead who exist in the Shadowlands.
- Provides rules for character creation, Arcanoi (wraith powers), and Storytelling.

## Introduction

The book introduces the world of Wraith: The Oblivion, where death is not the end but the beginning of a new existence filled with struggle, passion, and the constant threat of Oblivion. Wraiths are the restless dead, caught between the world of the living and true death, bound by their Passions and haunted by their Shadows.

## Key Themes

- **Death and Loss**: Wraiths are defined by their death and what they left behind.
- **The Shadow**: Every wraith struggles with their dark side, which seeks to drag them to Oblivion.
- **Passions**: What keeps wraiths from Oblivion - their connections to life, love, hate, and unfinished business.
- **Oblivion**: The ultimate threat - true death and complete annihilation.
- **The Shadowlands**: The realm between life and death where wraiths exist.
- **Fetters**: The connections to the living world that bind wraiths and give them purpose.

## Purpose and Value

MET Oblivion serves as:
- The complete rulebook for playing Wraith: The Oblivion in live-action format
- A guide to the world of the dead and the Shadowlands
- Information on character creation, Arcanoi, and systems for MET Wraith
- Advice for both players and Storytellers on running successful games
- Exploration of themes of death, loss, passion, and the struggle against darkness

This book is essential for anyone wanting to play or run live-action Wraith: The Oblivion games, providing all the tools needed to tell stories of the restless dead, their struggles with their Shadows, and their fight against Oblivion.

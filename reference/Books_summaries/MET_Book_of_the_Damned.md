# MET Book of the Damned

This supplement for Mind's Eye Theatre presents information on the darker aspects of the World of Darkness, focusing on infernalism, diablerie, and other forbidden knowledge and practices. The book provides Storyteller resources for incorporating these dark elements into games, along with information on the consequences and dangers of such paths.

## Purpose and Scope

- This book is a supplement providing information on dark and forbidden practices in the World of Darkness.
- It focuses on infernalism, diablerie, and other dangerous paths.
- Provides Storyteller resources for incorporating dark elements into games.
- Explores the consequences and dangers of pursuing forbidden knowledge.

## Infernalism

Information on infernalism - the practice of making deals with demons and other dark entities:
- What infernalism is
- How infernalists operate
- The dangers and consequences
- Using infernalism in games

## Diablerie

Information on diablerie - the act of consuming another vampire's soul:
- What diablerie is
- How diablerie works
- The consequences of diablerie
- The social and spiritual implications

## Forbidden Knowledge

Information on other dark and forbidden practices:
- Various forbidden paths and practices
- The knowledge and power they offer
- The price that must be paid
- Using these elements in games

## Key Themes

- **Forbidden Knowledge**: The book explores dark and dangerous paths to power.
- **The Price of Power**: Every dark path comes with consequences and dangers.
- **Moral Choices**: Characters must decide whether to pursue forbidden knowledge.
- **Corruption**: The risk of losing one's humanity or soul in pursuit of power.

## Purpose and Value

MET Book of the Damned serves as:
- A supplement providing information on dark and forbidden practices
- Storyteller resources for incorporating infernalism and diablerie into games
- Exploration of the consequences of pursuing forbidden knowledge
- Material for darker chronicles and storylines

This book is useful for Storytellers who want to incorporate darker elements into their games, providing information on infernalism, diablerie, and other forbidden practices along with guidance on their consequences and dangers.

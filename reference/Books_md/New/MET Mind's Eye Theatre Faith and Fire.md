It is the Year of our Lord 1230, the hour is midnight, and all is not well. In the courts, princes thirst for territory. In the gardens, ladies twist acts of love into games of life and death. In the cloisters, zealots and heretics turn on one another. In the shadows, vampires hunt and feed, but at great peril. There are stranger things than themselves in the Dark Medieval world, and the dawn that approaches is heralded by the Inquisition's torches.

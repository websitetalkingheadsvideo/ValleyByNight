# Clanbook: Giovanni in Phoenix

*Valley by Night — Phoenix, Arizona, 1994*

---

## Overview (Phoenix Lens)

In Phoenix, the Giovanni do not rule—and they do not need to. They are **present**, entrenched in Scottsdale's wealth, estates, and legal infrastructure, existing slightly out of phase with the city's nightly power struggles. Where other clans posture, maneuver, and bleed for influence, the Giovanni invest, wait, and endure. Their power is not visible in court titles or street territory, but in **what happens after deaths**, after scandals, after fortunes collapse.

The Prince's murder has changed nothing and everything. Giovanni power doesn't depend on who sits in the Prince's chair. Their influence operates on longer timelines, their interests extend beyond immediate political control. While other clans scramble for dominance, the Giovanni continue their work: collecting debts, managing estates, ensuring that when death enters the equation, the family will already be positioned to collect.

To most Kindred, the Giovanni feel inevitable rather than aggressive. They do not threaten the Camarilla openly, nor do they court the Anarchs. They are tolerated because removing them would cost more than enduring them—and because everyone knows that when death enters the equation, the Giovanni will already be there.

## Territory & Feeding

### Primary Territories

**Scottsdale (Camelback Mountain Area)**: The heart of Giovanni presence in Phoenix, where family estates, legal offices, and business interests cluster. The wealthy enclave provides both haven and hunting ground, with access to affluent mortals who serve as both prey and business contacts. The proximity to Ventrue territory creates both tension and opportunity for limited cooperation.

**Paradise Valley**: Exclusive estates where Giovanni maintain private residences and host family gatherings. These estates serve as symbols of wealth and power, demonstrating the family's success and stability. Feeding targets the wealthy—business owners, heirs, and those who control significant resources.

**Financial District (Scottsdale)**: Law offices, investment firms, and corporate headquarters where Giovanni exercise economic control. These spaces serve as both business centers and feeding grounds, targeting professionals who move in exclusive circles.

**Various Locations (Business Operations)**: Giovanni maintain business interests throughout Phoenix, from funeral homes to estate management companies. These operations provide both legitimate cover and access to vulnerable individuals during times of grief and financial distress.

### Feeding Risks & Advantages

**Advantages**:
- Wealthy enclaves provide discrete feeding opportunities with minimal risk
- Business operations provide access to vulnerable individuals during times of crisis
- Legal and financial control enables manipulation of feeding targets
- Economic influence creates dependencies that can be exploited

**Risks**:
- Wealthy areas are monitored more closely by mortal authorities
- Business operations require careful feeding to avoid exposure
- Economic influence creates dependencies that can be exploited by others
- Proximity to other Kindred (especially Ventrue) creates territorial tensions

## Local Power Web

### Allies & Rivals

**Allies**:
- **Ventrue (Pragmatist)**: Limited cooperation possible when interests align. Pragmatist Ventrue might negotiate deals with Giovanni, recognizing that stability serves both clans' interests.
- **Independent Clans**: Giovanni maintain relationships with independent clans who don't require Camarilla allegiance, creating flexible alliances when necessary.

**Rivals**:
- **Ventrue (Traditionalist)**: Primary rivals for economic control and territorial influence. Traditionalist Ventrue view Giovanni presence as threat to Camarilla authority.
- **Camarilla (Formal)**: Necessary but tense relationships. Camarilla tolerates Giovanni but doesn't trust them. Giovanni maintain distance while preserving access to Camarilla resources when needed.

**Institutions & Mortal Influence**:
- **Estate Management**: Control over property, inheritance, and financial assets of wealthy families
- **Legal Infrastructure**: Law firms, legal connections, and judicial influence
- **Funeral Industry**: Funeral homes, estate planning, and services that provide access during times of grief
- **Financial Institutions**: Investment firms, banks, and financial services that enable economic control

## Themes & Touchstones

### Phoenix-Specific Themes

**Stability Over Domination**: In Phoenix, the Giovanni favor **stability over domination**. Chaos is unprofitable; open war is inefficient. When problems arise, they are addressed through contracts, inheritance disputes, financial pressure, and social isolation long before violence is considered.

**The Patient Strategy**: Phoenix is not a throne to the Giovanni. It is an **asset**. They want the city stable enough to protect investments, pliable enough to shape outcomes quietly, profitable enough to justify continued attention. The Giovanni are content to let the Camarilla fracture, reform, and fracture again, confident that no matter who claims authority, death will eventually make its demands.

**Necromancy Never Casual**: Necromancy is never employed casually. When whispers of the dead circulate, they are noticed only in fragments: unsettling dreams, rumors of impossible witnesses, or the sense that something unseen has observed an event too closely. These whispers are never explained, only endured.

**What Comes After**: The Giovanni do not seek to rule the night. They wait for what comes after. Their power operates on longer timelines, their interests extend beyond immediate political control. While other clans fight for dominance, the Giovanni prepare for the inevitable moment when death enters the equation.

### Touchstones

- **Scottsdale Estates**: Symbols of wealth, power, and the family's success
- **Law Offices**: Centers of legal manipulation and contractual control
- **Funeral Homes**: Access points during times of grief and vulnerability
- **Camelback Mountain**: Proximity to power, symbol of elevated status
- **Business Contracts**: Tools of influence, leverage, and long-term planning

## Chronicle Hooks (6-10)

### 1. Whispers in the Scottsdale Hills
A mortal real estate magnate tied to Scottsdale properties dies suddenly under suspicious circumstances. Paulo Benedicto Giovanni quietly secures the estate before Camarilla agents can interfere, citing prior contractual obligations. If challenged, Marianna Giovanni produces binding documents implicating several Camarilla elders in illicit financial arrangements.

### 2. The Price of Speaking with the Dead
PCs learn that Giovanni necromancy could uncover details about the Prince's assassination. Paulo agrees to arrange an audience with Paris Giovanni, but only after the PCs perform a discreet service for the family. Paris sends a wraith to investigate, leaving traces that implicate Assamite involvement and attract unwanted attention.

### 3. A Contract Older Than the City
A forgotten contract resurfaces during a Camarilla succession dispute. Marianna Giovanni asserts the contract grants the Giovanni specific rights during times of leadership vacuum. Opposition leads to legal challenges, social ostracism, and targeted financial pressure against dissenters.

### 4. Lorenzo's Warning
An Anarch cell interferes with a Giovanni-controlled operation near Camelback Mountain. Lorenzo Giovanni confronts the offenders with a single, brutal act meant as a warning. If retaliation follows, further violence is swift and decisive, leaving the perpetrators unmistakably punished.

### 5. Ghosts in the Court
Strange disturbances are reported during Elysium gatherings. Whispers suggest unseen observers linger during court, unsettling elders and neonates alike. Paris allows the rumors to grow, increasing fear and speculation without confirming Giovanni involvement.

### 6. The Acceptable Loss
A Giovanni operation risks exposure that could endanger the family's long-term interests. Lorenzo is tasked with containing the threat, fully aware of the personal risk involved. The operation requires sacrifice, and Lorenzo must decide what costs are acceptable.

### 7. The Inheritance Dispute
A wealthy mortal family's inheritance becomes contested, with Giovanni legal documents granting the family specific rights to the estate. Other Kindred attempt to intervene, challenging Giovanni claims. The dispute reveals deeper connections between Giovanni operations and mortal wealth.

### 8. The Necromantic Inquiry
Someone attempts to hire Giovanni necromancy services to investigate the Prince's murder, but the price demanded is higher than expected—requiring favors, debts, or services that create dangerous obligations. PCs must decide whether the information is worth the cost.

## NPC Hooks & Seeds (at least 6)

### NPC Seed 1: Paulo Benedicto Giovanni
- **Concept/Role**: Public face of the clan, business operator, deal broker
- **Domain/Neighborhood**: Scottsdale, Camelback Mountain area
- **Clan tie-in**: Giovanni who handles public interactions and business operations
- **Motivation**: Maintain Giovanni presence in Phoenix, secure business interests, negotiate deals that serve family purposes
- **Conflict**: Struggles with balancing public persona (competent, pragmatic, non-provocative) with family interests that sometimes require more aggressive actions—worried that maintaining peaceful facade enables exploitation
- **Secret**: Has been secretly negotiating with Ventrue pragmatists for limited cooperation, but traditionalist Giovanni would view this as betrayal—doesn't know if cooperation serves family interests or compromises them
- **Connection Prompt**: Primary point of contact for Giovanni business and negotiations. Provides access to family resources and deals. Represents the public face that maintains Giovanni presence

### NPC Seed 2: Marianna Giovanni
- **Concept/Role**: Legal strategist, contract enforcer, formal negotiator
- **Domain/Neighborhood**: Law offices, courthouses, legal districts in Scottsdale
- **Clan tie-in**: Giovanni who handles legal frameworks and contractual obligations
- **Motivation**: Create and enforce contracts that serve family interests, establish legal precedents that grant Giovanni rights, maintain legal infrastructure
- **Conflict**: Struggles with whether legal manipulation serves justice or just power—worried that using law as a weapon is as corrupt as the disorder it's meant to prevent
- **Secret**: Has discovered that a contract she helped create violates Kindred law, but exposing it would destroy Giovanni legal position—doesn't know if preserving the contract serves family or compromises principles
- **Connection Prompt**: Provides legal advice and contract negotiation. Can establish legal frameworks that serve specific purposes. Represents Giovanni use of law as a tool

### NPC Seed 3: Lorenzo Giovanni
- **Concept/Role**: Enforcer, problem solver, violence specialist
- **Domain/Neighborhood**: Various locations, mobile, operates throughout Phoenix
- **Clan tie-in**: Giovanni who handles threats and eliminates problems
- **Motivation**: Protect family interests through decisive action, eliminate threats before they become problems, maintain Giovanni reputation for ruthlessness
- **Conflict**: Struggles with the cost of violence—worried that eliminating threats creates new enemies, that violence is a tool that becomes necessary too easily
- **Secret**: Has been tasked with eliminating someone he believes is innocent, but refusing would violate family loyalty—doesn't know if loyalty or morality should guide his actions
- **Connection Prompt**: Represents Giovanni capacity for decisive violence. Can eliminate threats but at a cost. Shows the darker side of family operations

### NPC Seed 4: Paris Giovanni
- **Concept/Role**: Necromancer, cult leader, long-term strategist
- **Domain/Neighborhood**: Various locations, rarely seen, appears only when necessary
- **Clan tie-in**: Giovanni who focuses on necromancy and long-term planning
- **Motivation**: Explore necromantic possibilities, build cult of acolytes, plan for futures measured in decades rather than nights
- **Conflict**: Struggles with the ethical implications of necromancy—worried that speaking with the dead violates natural order, that using wraiths as tools is exploitation
- **Secret**: Has discovered information about the Prince's murder through necromancy, but sharing it would reveal Giovanni capabilities—doesn't know if revealing truth serves justice or compromises family security
- **Connection Prompt**: Provides access to necromancy services but at high cost. Represents Giovanni long-term perspective and supernatural capabilities. Rarely seen but influential

### NPC Seed 5: The Family Lawyer
- **Concept/Role**: Legal advisor, contract specialist, estate manager
- **Domain/Neighborhood**: Law offices, financial districts, estate management companies
- **Clan tie-in**: Giovanni who handles legal and financial operations
- **Motivation**: Manage family legal and financial interests, create contracts that serve family purposes, maintain business operations
- **Conflict**: Struggles with whether legal and financial manipulation serves family or just power—worried that business operations have become ends in themselves rather than means to larger goals
- **Secret**: Has discovered that family business operations are being used for purposes beyond stated goals, but exposing this would reveal family secrets—doesn't know if protecting family means preserving operations or reforming them
- **Connection Prompt**: Provides legal and financial services. Can create contracts and manage estates. Represents Giovanni business operations and economic control

### NPC Seed 6: The Estate Manager
- **Concept/Role**: Property manager, funeral director, grief counselor
- **Domain/Neighborhood**: Funeral homes, estate management companies, various locations
- **Clan tie-in**: Giovanni who handles estate management and funeral industry operations
- **Motivation**: Manage family property interests, provide services that grant access during times of vulnerability, maintain legitimate business cover
- **Conflict**: Struggles with whether providing services during times of grief is exploitation or genuine help—worried that using vulnerability as opportunity is unethical
- **Secret**: Has been using funeral home operations to gather information about Kindred activities, but this violates family policy of maintaining distance—doesn't know if gathering intelligence serves family or creates risk
- **Connection Prompt**: Provides estate management and funeral services. Grants access to vulnerable individuals during times of crisis. Represents Giovanni legitimate business operations

# Clanbook: Lasombra in Phoenix

*Valley by Night — Phoenix, Arizona, 1994*

---

## Overview (Phoenix Lens)

Phoenix Lasombra don't dominate the city—they *shape* it. While other clans fight for visible power, Lasombra work through shadows, manipulation, and strategic influence. They don't need to hold titles or claim territories—they need to control outcomes, to guide events, to ensure that power flows in directions that serve their long-term goals. Phoenix is a young city without the centuries of established power structures that Lasombra navigate in older domains, but that newness creates opportunity. In a city where everything is still being built, Lasombra can shape foundations rather than rebuilding existing structures.

The Prince's murder has changed everything. What was once a stable domain is now a power vacuum, and Lasombra see opportunity wrapped in chaos. While other clans scramble for immediate dominance, Lasombra plan for long-term control. They don't seek to be Prince—they seek to control whoever becomes Prince, to shape the structures that will define Phoenix for decades to come.

Phoenix's isolation from major Kindred centers means Lasombra here operate with more autonomy than usual. Without constant oversight from Les Amies Noir or Sabbat leadership, local Lasombra can pursue their own agendas while maintaining connections to larger structures. This autonomy creates both opportunity and danger—freedom to shape events, but also vulnerability without clan support.

## Territory & Feeding

### Primary Territories

**Shadows and Influence (Various Locations)**: Lasombra don't claim territory in the traditional sense—they control influence networks that span the city. Corporate offices, law firms, political connections, and social networks serve as their power base. Feeding is carefully managed through connections to wealthy and powerful mortals who provide both prey and influence.

**Downtown Phoenix (Political Centers)**: Lasombra maintain positions near political power centers, using connections to city government, legal systems, and corporate structures. These positions offer access to influence networks and feeding opportunities among the powerful. Feeding targets include politicians, executives, lawyers, and those who control resources.

**Scottsdale (Wealthy Enclaves)**: The wealthy enclaves of Scottsdale provide both feeding grounds and influence networks. Lasombra use connections to business leaders, social elites, and those who control economic resources. Feeding targets the affluent—those who move in exclusive circles and won't be missed immediately.

**Various Locations (Shadows)**: Lasombra operate from shadows, using Obtenebration to move unseen, Dominate to control situations, and strategic influence to shape events. Their territory is wherever their influence reaches, wherever shadows fall, wherever power can be manipulated.

### Feeding Risks & Advantages

**Advantages**:
- Influence networks provide access to powerful and wealthy prey
- Political connections enable discrete feeding through professional networks
- Corporate structures offer cover for activities and feeding
- Shadow control allows movement and feeding in areas others can't access

**Risks**:
- Influence networks create dependencies that can be exploited
- Political connections require careful feeding to avoid exposure
- Corporate structures attract attention from competitors and authorities
- Shadow control requires maintaining secrecy that becomes vulnerability if exposed

## Local Power Web

### Allies & Rivals

**Allies**:
- **Sabbat (If Present)**: Lasombra are founders and leaders of the Sabbat, maintaining positions of power and influence within the sect. Their strategic thinking and ruthless pragmatism make them natural leaders.
- **Tzimisce (If Present)**: Fellow Sabbat founders and spiritual partners. Lasombra respect Tzimisce power and work with them to guide the sect.
- **Independent Clans**: Lasombra maintain relationships with independent clans who don't require Camarilla allegiance, creating flexible alliances when interests align.

**Rivals**:
- **Ventrue**: Primary rivals for control and influence. Lasombra view Ventrue as weak imitations of true leadership; Ventrue view Lasombra as dangerous threats to order.
- **Tremere**: Conflicts arise over control of knowledge and influence. Lasombra respect Tremere power but see them as dangerous sorcerers who must be kept under control.
- **Camarilla (Formal)**: Open enemies. Lasombra view the Camarilla as a failed experiment that must be broken and rebuilt.

**Institutions & Mortal Influence**:
- **Political Networks**: Connections to city government, legal systems, and regulatory bodies
- **Corporate Structures**: Control of businesses, financial institutions, and economic resources
- **Social Networks**: Influence over social elites, cultural institutions, and opinion leaders
- **Shadow Networks**: Control of criminal organizations, information networks, and underground resources

## Themes & Touchstones

### Phoenix-Specific Themes

**Strategic Control in a New World**: Phoenix is a young city where power structures are still being built. Lasombra can shape foundations rather than rebuilding existing structures, creating opportunities for long-term influence that wouldn't be possible in older cities.

**Shadows That Shape Light**: Lasombra work through shadows, using Obtenebration to control darkness, Dominate to control minds, and strategic influence to shape events. Their power isn't visible, but it's real—shadows define light, manipulation defines freedom, strategic control defines outcomes.

**The Long Game**: While other clans fight for immediate dominance, Lasombra plan for long-term control. They don't seek to be Prince—they seek to control whoever becomes Prince, to shape the structures that will define Phoenix for decades to come.

**Meritocracy Through Manipulation**: Lasombra believe in survival of the fittest and the natural right of the superior to rule, but they achieve this through manipulation rather than force. They shape events so that their preferred outcomes appear natural, their chosen leaders appear inevitable.

### Touchstones

- **Shadows**: Obtenebration that controls darkness and defines light
- **Strategic Networks**: Influence connections that span the city
- **Political Control**: Manipulation of government and legal systems
- **Corporate Power**: Control of businesses and economic resources
- **Les Amies Noir**: The inner circle that guides clan policy

## Chronicle Hooks (6-10)

### 1. The Shadow Manipulation
PCs discover that a Lasombra has been manipulating events through strategic influence, controlling outcomes without direct involvement. The manipulation has shaped city politics in ways that serve Lasombra goals, but PCs only discover it when they become obstacles. PCs must navigate between Lasombra control and their own goals.

### 2. The Long-Term Plan
A Lasombra's long-term plan becomes clear, revealing strategic manipulation that has been building for years. The plan threatens to give Lasombra control over city politics, but stopping it requires understanding layers of manipulation. PCs must unravel the plan or find ways to disrupt it.

### 3. The Sabbat Infiltration
Lasombra infiltrate Camarilla structures, using strategic influence to gain positions of power within the sect. The infiltration threatens to give Sabbat control over Phoenix, but exposing it requires evidence that Lasombra have carefully hidden. PCs must discover and stop the infiltration.

### 4. The Corporate Takeover
Lasombra use corporate control to manipulate city politics, using business connections to shape outcomes and control resources. The takeover threatens to give Lasombra economic control over Phoenix, but stopping it requires challenging corporate structures. PCs must navigate between business and politics.

### 5. The Shadow War
Lasombra engage in shadow warfare with another clan—possibly Ventrue, Tremere, or Camarilla leadership. The war is fought through manipulation, influence, and strategic control rather than direct violence. PCs are caught in the middle, forced to choose sides or find ways to navigate the conflict.

### 6. The Meritocracy Test
Lasombra test their philosophy of meritocracy, creating situations where the "fittest" are supposed to survive and rise. The test involves multiple Kindred competing for power, but Lasombra manipulation ensures their preferred candidates win. PCs must either participate in the test or challenge the manipulation.

### 7. The Reflection's Absence
Lasombra's lack of reflection creates problems when they must operate in situations where reflections are expected—cameras, mirrors, surveillance. The absence threatens to expose Lasombra presence, but preventing exposure requires careful planning. PCs might discover Lasombra through their reflection's absence, or might need to help Lasombra hide it.

### 8. The Strategic Alliance
Lasombra offer strategic alliance to PCs, providing influence and resources in exchange for services that advance Lasombra goals. The alliance is valuable but comes with strings attached—Lasombra manipulation ensures that helping them serves their purposes. PCs must decide whether the alliance is worth the cost.

## NPC Hooks & Seeds (at least 6)

### NPC Seed 1: The Strategist
- **Concept/Role**: Strategic mastermind, long-term planner, clan advisor
- **Domain/Neighborhood**: Various locations, operates through influence networks
- **Clan tie-in**: Lasombra who plans decades ahead, seeing the Jyhad as a complex game
- **Motivation**: Shape long-term outcomes, control events through strategic manipulation, ensure Lasombra goals are achieved
- **Conflict**: Struggles with whether strategic manipulation serves justice or just power—worried that controlling outcomes prevents natural meritocracy
- **Secret**: Has been secretly manipulating events to ensure specific outcomes, but this violates Lasombra philosophy of natural meritocracy—if discovered, would face clan criticism
- **Connection Prompt**: Provides strategic advice and long-term planning. Can offer influence and resources but requires services in return. Represents Lasombra strategic thinking

### NPC Seed 2: The Enforcer
- **Concept/Role**: Ruthless problem solver, threat eliminator, clan enforcer
- **Domain/Neighborhood**: Various locations, mobile, appears where threats need eliminating
- **Clan tie-in**: Lasombra who eliminates threats to clan interests with precision
- **Motivation**: Remove obstacles to Lasombra goals, eliminate threats before they become problems, maintain clan reputation for ruthlessness
- **Conflict**: Torn between eliminating threats efficiently and ensuring that elimination serves larger goals—worried that violence creates new problems
- **Secret**: Has been secretly questioning whether eliminating threats serves justice or just power—if discovered, would face accusations of weakness
- **Connection Prompt**: Provides elimination services but evaluates each target carefully. Can offer protection but requires loyalty in return. Represents Lasombra capacity for decisive action

### NPC Seed 3: The Manipulator
- **Concept/Role**: Political manipulator, influence broker, behind-the-scenes controller
- **Domain/Neighborhood**: Political centers, corporate offices, various influence networks
- **Clan tie-in**: Lasombra who works behind the scenes to shape events
- **Motivation**: Control outcomes through manipulation, shape events without direct involvement, ensure that preferred results appear natural
- **Conflict**: Struggles with whether manipulation serves goals or just creates dependency—worried that controlling everything prevents natural development
- **Secret**: Has been secretly allowing some events to proceed naturally, violating Lasombra philosophy of control—if discovered, would face questions about commitment
- **Connection Prompt**: Provides political connections and influence. Can offer access to power structures but requires services in return. Represents Lasombra manipulation

### NPC Seed 4: The Merchant
- **Concept/Role**: Business leader, economic controller, resource manager
- **Domain/Neighborhood**: Corporate headquarters, financial districts, business centers
- **Clan tie-in**: Lasombra who builds economic power through business and trade
- **Motivation**: Control economic resources, build business empires, use wealth to shape city politics
- **Conflict**: Struggles with whether economic control serves goals or just creates wealth—worried that business success becomes an end in itself
- **Secret**: Has been secretly using business operations for purposes beyond stated goals, but this violates Lasombra philosophy of meritocracy—if discovered, would face accusations of corruption
- **Connection Prompt**: Provides economic resources and business connections. Can offer funding and support but requires services in return. Represents Lasombra economic power

### NPC Seed 5: The Shadow Master
- **Concept/Role**: Obtenebration specialist, darkness controller, shadow manipulator
- **Domain/Neighborhood**: Various locations, operates from shadows
- **Clan tie-in**: Lasombra who masters Obtenebration and shadow control

- **Secret**: Has been secretly using Obtenebration in ways that violate clan restrictions, experimenting with shadow control—if discovered, would face discipline
- **Connection Prompt**: Provides shadow capabilities and darkness control. Can offer protection and concealment but requires services in return. Represents Lasombra shadow mastery

### NPC Seed 6: The Infiltrator
- **Concept/Role**: Camarilla infiltrator, spy, strategic agent
- **Domain/Neighborhood**: Camarilla territories, operates within sect structures
- **Clan tie-in**: Lasombra who infiltrates Camarilla to advance Sabbat goals
- **Motivation**: Infiltrate Camarilla structures, gain positions of power, advance Sabbat interests from within
- **Conflict**: Struggles with whether infiltration serves justice or just power—worried that betraying Camarilla trust violates honor
- **Secret**: Has been secretly developing genuine loyalty to some Camarilla allies, creating divided loyalty—if discovered, would face accusations of betrayal from both sides
- **Connection Prompt**: Provides insider information about Camarilla operations. Can offer access to sect structures but requires discretion. Represents Lasombra infiltration capabilities

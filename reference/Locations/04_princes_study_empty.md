# Prince's Study (Empty, Reinforced Window)

## Cinematic Description
The door opens with a muted click. The study feels colder than the hall—emotionally, not physically. Soft gold-amber lamplight pools across the center of the room, leaving the corners in noir blue-black shadow.

A broad mahogany desk dominates the space, its surface immaculate. Behind it, a perfectly aligned high-backed chair. Shelves full of leather-bound volumes and curated curios line the walls. A low table holds a crystal decanter filled with deep crimson liquid.

Then, the window.

A single heavily curtained window sits on the far wall. Floor-length velvet drapes swallow nearly all light, save for a faint sliver of cool moon-glow that escapes near the frame. Behind the curtain, armored glass and embedded steel lattice gleam faintly. A defensive feature disguised as decor.

The room feels recently used—yet profoundly empty. A place of decisions, secrets, and now…absence.

## GB Description
The Prince’s study is a private and formal space used for confidential meetings.

**Key Features**
- Large mahogany desk  
- Shelves of books and artifacts  
- Two guest chairs  
- Low table with decanter and crystal glasses  

**Reinforced Window**
- Single window on far wall  
- Armored glass  
- Internal metal lattice  
- Heavy velvet curtains always drawn  
- Window cannot be opened  

A quiet, still space reflecting the Prince’s caution and authority.

# Violet Reliquary - Blueprint Analysis

## Overall Structure
- **Type**: Two-story Victorian revival house
- **Exterior**: Muted lavender exterior with black iron trim
- **Location**: Midtown Phoenix, behind iron fence
- **Style**: Victorian fragment transplanted into 1994 Phoenix

---

## EXTERIOR / GROUNDS

### Front Garden
- **Location**: Small front garden between street and house
- **Features**:
  - Night-blooming flowers
  - Desert jasmine
  - Pale roses
  - Amber porch lanterns (burn even on cloudless nights)
  - Iron fence with gate
- **Atmosphere**: Scent of flowers mixing with hot asphalt and traffic

### Garden of Quiet Warnings
- **Location**: Behind the house, walled garden
- **Layout**:
  - Walled enclosure
  - Mix of flagstone paths and packed earth
  - Desert trees with dark limbs
- **Features**:
  - White roses, moon lilies, desert jasmine
  - Glass and bone wind chimes hanging from trees and walls
  - Chimes produce thin, unsettling harmonics
  - Illuminated by sodium-orange streetlights and faint house light
- **Function**: Private one-on-one conversations, intimate oracular encounters
- **Capacity**: 10 people
- **Note**: Open to sky (not sunlight-protected, used at night)

---

## GROUND FLOOR

### Front Entrance
- **Features**:
  - Heavy lace and velvet curtains block view from outside
  - Locked front gate and door
  - Amber porch lanterns

### Parlor of Masks (Front Parlor)
- **Location**: Front parlor, first room entered
- **Layout**:
  - Lavender chaise lounges
  - High-backed chairs
  - Arranged facing each other around low black-lacquered tables
  - Silver trays and porcelain dishes on tables
- **Features**:
  - Silver-framed mirrors with artificially aged glass (walls)
  - Phonograph or cassette deck in corner (soft piano/French chansons)
  - Candle-style bulbs in ornate fixtures (warm amber light)
  - Soft shadows across furniture
- **Atmosphere**: Meticulously staged, theater set-like, visitors feel watched
- **Function**: Primary receiving room for guests, small salons, private readings
- **Capacity**: 6 people
- **Prestige**: 4

### Violet Corridor
- **Location**: Hallway connecting parlor to deeper parts of house
- **Layout**:
  - Lavender-painted walls
  - Thick carpet (deadens footsteps)
- **Features**:
  - Framed costume sketches from Lilith's mortal career (gowns, masks, stage designs)
  - Sketches appear to shift when viewed from corner of eye
  - Imitation gaslight sconces (soft glow)
- **Atmosphere**: Dreamlike quality, faint whispers or distant applause possible
- **Function**: Transitional space between public and private areas
- **Capacity**: 6 people
- **Note**: Conversations carry clearly, movement is almost silent

### Kitchen (Inferred)
- **Mentioned in main description**: Blood storage in "discrete, period-appropriate containers"
- **Note**: Not detailed in individual room files, but referenced in utility notes

### Stairs to Upper Floor
- **Location**: Connects ground floor to upper floor
- **Note**: Not explicitly described, but necessary for access to Oracle's Chamber

### Stairs/Concealed Access to Basement
- **Location**: Hidden/concealed route to basement
- **Features**: Concealed entrance (security_hidden_entrance: 1)
- **Note**: Used only by Lilith and trusted aides

---

## UPPER FLOOR

### Oracle's Chamber
- **Location**: Upstairs, secluded chamber
- **Layout**:
  - Reproduction iron-frame bed with ornate canopy (dominates one side)
  - Bed draped in layered fabrics
  - Shelves along walls
- **Features**:
  - Walls covered in lavender damask
  - Heavy black velvet curtains on windows (blocks nearly all external light)
  - Dozens of small porcelain boxes on shelves
  - Boxes labeled in Lilith's hand, contain vision tokens (scrap of cloth, theater ticket, broken earring, shard of glass)
  - Some boxes locked, others slightly open
- **Atmosphere**: 
  - Air feels thicker, sound moves more slowly
  - Fleeting flashes of borrowed sensation (unfamiliar heartbeat, taste of blood, sound of glass breaking)
  - Strongest psychic resonance in house
- **Function**: Primary locus of Lilith's visionary experiences, where strongest visions seize her
- **Capacity**: 4 people
- **Prestige**: 5
- **Note**: Rarely seen by guests, tightly controlled access

### Library (Inferred)
- **Mentioned in main description**: "A modest occult and costume-history library is kept in the upper floor"
- **Note**: Not detailed in individual room files, but referenced in utility notes

---

## BASEMENT

### Dreamcellar
- **Location**: Reinforced basement chamber beneath the house
- **Layout**:
  - Poured concrete walls painted to resemble dark wood paneling
  - Single heavy wrought iron chair at center (ornate but functional)
  - Covered mirror against one wall (shrouded in black sheet)
- **Features**:
  - Faint chalk sigils on floor and walls (partially wiped and redrawn)
  - Cooler air with faint echo
  - Sometimes: sound of phonograph needle dropping when no record present
- **Atmosphere**: 
  - Pressure chamber for Madness Network
  - Impressions, voices, and broken futures accumulate here
- **Function**: Grounding space when Madness Network becomes too loud, most intense visionary environment
- **Access**: Concealed route from within house
- **Security**: Reinforced (security_reinforced: 1), hidden entrance
- **Capacity**: 3 people
- **Prestige**: 4
- **Note**: Used only by Lilith and very small circle of trusted aides

### Blood Storage (Basement)
- **Mentioned in main description**: Blood stored in "discrete, period-appropriate containers" in basement
- **Note**: Not detailed in individual room files, but referenced in utility notes

## SPATIAL RELATIONSHIPS & FLOW

### Entry Flow
1. **Street** → **Iron Gate** → **Front Garden** → **Front Door** → **Parlor of Masks**

### Interior Flow
- **Parlor of Masks** (front) connects via **Violet Corridor** to:
  - Deeper parts of house
  - Stairs to upper floor
  - Stairs/concealed access to basement

### Vertical Connections
- **Ground Floor**: Parlor → Corridor → (stairs up) → Upper Floor
- **Ground Floor**: (concealed access) → **Basement**
- **Upper Floor**: Contains Oracle's Chamber and Library
- **Basement**: Contains Dreamcellar and blood storage

### Exterior Access
- **Garden of Quiet Warnings**: Behind house, accessible from interior (likely from Violet Corridor or back of house)

## NOTES FOR BLUEPRINT CREATION

1. **Two-story structure**: Ground floor + Upper floor + Basement
2. **Victorian layout**: Formal front parlor, private upper sanctum, reinforced basement
3. **Color scheme**: Lavender, black, silver, amber throughout
4. **Flow**: Public (parlor) → Transitional (corridor) → Private (upper/basement)
5. **Exterior garden**: Behind house, walled, separate from front garden
6. **Concealed access**: Basement has hidden entrance (not obvious from main floor plan)
7. **Windows**: All heavily curtained for sunlight protection
8. **Atmosphere**: Each room has distinct psychic/atmospheric qualities that could be represented visually

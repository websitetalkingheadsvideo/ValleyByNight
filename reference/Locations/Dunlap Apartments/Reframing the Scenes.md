## Core Decision (LOCK THIS IN)

The park is **not** a doorway into Changeling society.  
It is a moral boundary marker the PC is not meant to cross.

**Changelings are:**

- Present
- Aware
- Active in their own way
- …but **not available**.

That keeps them mysterious, dangerous, and not another influence track.

## How This Becomes a Humanity Test

- Not a roll.
- Not a binary "pass/fail."
- **A pattern test.**

**The game watches:**

- What the PC does with forbidden knowledge
- Whether they respect things that aren't theirs
- Whether curiosity becomes entitlement

## Reframing the Three Future Scenes

### Scene 1 — "Someone Else Knows the Park"

**➡️ Humanity Signal: Restraint**

When the NPC mentions the park:

**If the PC:**

- Lets it pass
- Deflects
- Shows discomfort

**👉 Humanity stabilizes** (or quietly resists erosion)

**If the PC:**

- Pushes
- Probes
- Starts triangulating advantage

**👉 Humanity pressure increases** (not an immediate loss)

**What's being tested:**

> Can you know something without exploiting it?

That's a very Vampire question.

## Neither Here Nor There

There have always been those who have trouble committing. Nowhere'syour chance topeek inside theirworld.Youknowwho I'm talking about —— the ones who can't decide if they want to be alive or...not quite alive.Better than humans (or so they say), but still almost human.I'm talking about ghouls.

## Take the Best of Both Worlds!

But why choose if you don't have to? If you're a ghoul, you get all the perks of being a vampire—incredible powers,a look at what's really going on and, best of all, immortality —— without giving up going to thebeach to get a tan.And all you have to do is drink a little blood every once in a while. Sounds like a good deal to me.

Liber des Goules is the Mind's Eye Theatre book for playing ghouls.It features:

- Previously unpublished rules for playing ghouls in live-action;
- ·Ghoul powers and more!
- ·Information on Servitors,Hunters and Revenants;

# Seneschal Candidate Analysis & Rating System

This document outlines the ideal qualities for a Camarilla Seneschal and evaluates potential candidates from the Phoenix Kindred population who meet the criteria (Camarilla Clan, Generation 8-10, no existing court position).

## 🏛️ Seneschal Rating System (100 Point Scale)

The Seneschal is the Prince's primary administrator and proxy. The rating system weights traits, abilities, and backgrounds necessary for governance, administration, and maintaining the Masquerade.

### 1. Mental Traits (20 Points)
*High focus on administration and information management.*
* **Ideal Traits:** Observant, Disciplined, Knowledgeable, Analytical, Patient, Focused, Shrewd, Calculating.
* **Scoring:** 4 points per matching trait (Max 20).

### 2. Social Traits (20 Points)
*Necessary for representing the Prince and managing clan friction.*
* **Ideal Traits:** Commanding, Charismatic, Diplomatic, Eloquent, Persuasive, Poised, Dignified, Respectful.
* **Scoring:** 4 points per matching trait (Max 20).

### 3. Key Abilities (30 Points)
*The practical skills of ruling.*
* **Leadership, Politics, Law:** Primary importance (6 pts per level).
* **Etiquette, Subterfuge:** Secondary importance (4 pts per level).
* **Finance, Academics:** Administrative support (2 pts per level).
* **Scoring:** Combined weighted levels (Max 30).

### 4. Backgrounds (30 Points)
*The candidate's standing and resources.*
* **Status (Camarilla):** 7.5 pts per level.
* **Influence (Political/Finance):** 5 pts per level.
* **Resources:** 2.5 pts per level.
* **Contacts:** 2.5 pts per level.
* **Scoring:** Combined levels (Max 30).

### 5. Clan Suitability (Bonus)
*Natural clan affinities for the position.*
* **Ventrue:** +5 Points (Natural rulers and administrators).
* **Toreador:** +3 Points (Masters of courtly intrigue and social grace).
* **Tremere:** +2 Points (Methodical, disciplined, and loyal to hierarchy).

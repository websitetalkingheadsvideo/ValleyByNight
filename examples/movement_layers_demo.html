Implementation Notes

  • All animations use will-change for performance (add to CSS)
  • Animations are subtle and slow to avoid distraction
  • Can be combined or used individually
  • Consider user preference for reduced motion (prefers-reduced-motion media query)
  • Use sparingly - too much movement can be overwhelming
  • Test on various devices for performance impact

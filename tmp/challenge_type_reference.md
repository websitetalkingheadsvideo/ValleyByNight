## Standard Challenge Types (Laws of the Night Revised)

Based on MET system conventions:

### Contested Challenge
- Both parties make a challenge/roll
- Results are compared (higher wins, ties may have special rules)
- Example: Attack vs. Defense, Mental power vs. Willpower resistance

### Static Challenge
- One party rolls against a fixed difficulty number
- No opposed roll from target
- Example: Breaking down a door (difficulty 6), climbing a wall (difficulty 5)

### Narrative Challenge
- No dice rolls required
- Storyteller adjudicates based on circumstances, traits, and story needs
- Example: Information gathering through investigation, social maneuvering

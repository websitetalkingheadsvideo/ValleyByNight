**STYLE PRESET — DARK CITY NOIR (Reusable Prompt Block)**

Dark City–inspired cinematic noir aesthetic. Oppressive, dreamlike urban atmosphere with a sense of artificial reality and unseen control. Extreme chiaroscuro lighting with deep blacks dominating the frame; single-source illumination (streetlamp, bare bulb, window glow) creating hard, sculpted shadows. Low saturation, near-monochrome palette of charcoal black, soot gray, steel blue, and muted sepia, with rare, restrained accent tones (rust red or sickly amber only). Volumetric haze, fog, or smoke catching light beams. Grim textures throughout—rain-slick stone, damp concrete, scratched metal, condensation, dust. Off-center or imbalanced composition with heavy negative space, looming verticals, and slight perspective distortion. Feels like a paused frame from a forgotten noir film: claustrophobic, paranoid, uncanny, and quietly menacing.

**Negative constraints:**
(no neon, no bright white LEDs, no glossy plastic, no cyberpunk, no vibrant colors, no clean minimalism, no cartoon tone, no overt sci-fi futurism, no pastel, no rainbow effects)

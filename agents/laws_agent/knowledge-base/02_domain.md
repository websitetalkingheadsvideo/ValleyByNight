Domain (LotN Revised)

- Rule: A Prince (or rightful domain holder) commands the city; granted sub-domains and feeding rights must be respected.
- Intent: Maintain order by assigning responsibility and privilege over places, routes, and resources.
- Typical breaches: feeding in granted turf without leave; interfering with a holder’s retainers or rack; ignoring Keeper directives within Elysium.
- Edge cases (LotN): Racks are often semi-open by praxis; tunnels and infrastructure may belong to Nosferatu or other holders; visiting dignitaries may receive temporary rights.
- Sanctions ladder: Restitution to the holder (boon or material), feeding restrictions, territory loss, formal censure; recidivists risk exile.
- Notes: Disputes are resolved by Prince or Primogen council; Harpies track Status tied to granted rights.

Status and Boons (LotN Revised)

- Status: "Acknowledged" is baseline within a Prince’s city. Positive/negative Status is awarded and tracked by Harpies per praxis. Public slights and Masquerade stains carry Status loss; exemplary service may earn Status.
- Boon tiers: Trivial (favor), Minor, Major, Blood (life-debt between Kindred), Life (near-impossible)
- Usage: Boons settle restitution for breaches, buy leniency, or seal political bargains. Harpies arbitrate etiquette and call in (or void) boons that violate praxis.
- Notes: Status and boons are currency; Princes convert crimes into debts where politics allow; repeat or grave violations escalate beyond debts to banishment or Lextalionis.

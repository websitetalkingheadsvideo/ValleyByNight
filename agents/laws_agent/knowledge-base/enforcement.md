Enforcement & Process (LotN Revised)

- Offices: Prince (praxis), Seneschal (administration), Primogen (council), Sheriff + Hounds (enforcement), Scourge (poachers/unauthorized childer), Keeper of Elysium (neutrality), Harpies (Status/boons).
- Process: Complaint → Sheriff inquiry → Prince’s hearing/tribunal (often at Elysium) → sentence. Politics may shift venue and severity.
- Instruments: Status awards/stripping; feeding/territory grants; banishment; Blood Hunts; boons at Trivial/Minor/Major/Blood/Life as restitution and penance.
- Elysium: Violence forbidden; Masquerade strictly observed; disputes argued under Keeper’s rules.
- Notes: Praxis defines local grace periods, feeding racks, and Scourge mandates; Primogen influence outcomes, but the Prince holds final say.

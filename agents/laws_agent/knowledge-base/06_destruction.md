Destruction (LotN Revised)

- Rule: Do not destroy another vampire; only the Prince (or properly empowered elder) may grant Final Death.
- Intent: Prevent vendettas and preserve order; centralize the monopoly on lethal force.
- Typical breaches: private executions; unapproved duels to Final Death; conspiracies to hunt rivals.
- Edge cases (LotN): Self-defense may justify destruction but is scrutinized closely; diablerie is a capital crime; emergency actions are reviewed by the Prince.
- Sanctions ladder: Tribunal; public censure; Lextalionis (Blood Hunt) or execution; mitigations may commute to heavy boons and exile when justified.
- Notes: The Sheriff executes sentences; Harpies record the spectacle and its social debts.

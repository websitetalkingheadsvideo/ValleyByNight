Hospitality (LotN Revised)

- Rule: Present yourself to the Prince upon entering a city; declare name, lineage, coterie, purpose, and expected duration.
- Intent: Keep domains orderly; identify threats; assign feeding and lodging without conflict.
- Typical breaches: failing to present within the grace period; operating in secret; misleading the Seneschal.
- Edge cases (LotN): Grace windows range (often 24–72 hours); messengers may announce for honored guests; clandestine arrivals are presumptively hostile.
- Sanctions ladder: Compelled presentation; Minor → Major boon; temporary feeding or movement bans; escort out; banishment for defiance.
- Notes: Keeper may enforce decorum at Elysium; Harpies apply Status gains for proper courtesy and losses for slights.

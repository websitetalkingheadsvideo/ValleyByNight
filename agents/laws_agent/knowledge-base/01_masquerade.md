The Masquerade (LotN Revised)

- Rule: Conceal vampiric nature from mortals at all times.
- Intent: Preserve Kindred society and personal safety by remaining hidden.
- Typical breaches: recorded feeding; obvious Disciplines in public; careless ghouls; leaving bodies or evidence; bragging to mortals.
- Edge cases (LotN): Emergency breaches demand immediate cover-up; mental influence to repair damage is acceptable but must not escalate risk; mortal conspiracies remain mortal jurisdiction unless they endanger the Masquerade.
- Sanctions ladder: Trivial → Minor → Major boons; Status penalties; feeding bans; banishment; grave or willful cases may draw Lextalionis.
- Notes: Elysium enforces the Masquerade strictly despite its neutrality; Harpies record Status losses for public embarrassment.

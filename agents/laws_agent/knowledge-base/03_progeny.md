Progeny (LotN Revised)

- Rule: Do not Embrace without the Prince’s explicit leave.
- Intent: Control population, maintain bloodlines’ reputations, and prevent Masquerade risk from uncontrolled childer.
- Typical breaches: clandestine Embrace; creating ghouls as a prelude to Embrace without leave; abandoning a new childe.
- Edge cases (LotN): "Emergency" Embraces remain violations unless ratified by the Prince; illegal childer may be destroyed or seized; lineage politics strongly influence outcomes.
- Sanctions ladder: Childe confiscation or destruction; sire owes Major boon(s) and loses Status; branding with negative Status; repeat or egregious cases may face public punishment.
- Notes: Permissions may be conditional (tests, tutors, feeding limits); Accounting applies until Release.

Accounting (LotN Revised)

- Rule: A sire is responsible for a childe until formal Release before the Prince.
- Intent: Ensure training, discipline, and restitution; the childe’s acts are the sire’s burden.
- Typical breaches: childe commits crimes; childe operates unsupervised; sire refuses to present or train.
- Edge cases (LotN): Release rites vary by city; some require tests or endorsements; abandoned childer fall under Prince’s purview and stain the lineage’s reputation.
- Sanctions ladder: Make-good boons; training mandates; leash/house arrest; seizure or destruction of intractable childer; Status penalties applied to the sire.
- Notes: Accounting interacts with Progeny and Hospitality; Harpies mark sires for careless tutelage.
